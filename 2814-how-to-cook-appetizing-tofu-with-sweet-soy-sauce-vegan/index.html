Tofu with Sweet Soy Sauce *Vegan step by step

  1. Cut up fried tofu into small pieces. Chop garlics, one onion, chilies, and tomato. Cut another onion into 2 pieces and slice them thinly. Add 1 tbsp water into tamarind and squeeze it well with the water..
  2. Pre-heat cooking oil into saute pan, add chopped onion and garlics, saute them until turn golden, add chilies, tomatoes, sliced onion, mix well, cook until they turn soft. Add tamarind water, bayleaves and kaffir lime leaves, and salt, mix well..
  3. Add tofu, mix well. Add sweet soy sauce and water, stir until the water reduces..
  4. Ready to serve with rice or bread..
