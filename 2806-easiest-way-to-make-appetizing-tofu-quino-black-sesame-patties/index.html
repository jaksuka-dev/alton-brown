Ingredients of Tofu Quino Black Sesame Patties

  1. You need 1 cup of cooked Quinoa.
  2. You need 1 pack of Firm Tofu.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of Baby Bok Choy.
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup of Roast Black Sesame.
  5. You need 1 cup of Shedded Carrots.
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp of corn starch.
  7. You need 1 tbsp of Soy Sauce.
  8. You need 1/2 tsp of Salt.
  9. You need 1/2 tsp of Pepper.

Tofu Quino Black Sesame Patties instructions

  1. Wash and chop Baby Bok Choy into small pieces and set aside.
  2. Put the firm tofu into a big bowl.
  3. Add Soy Sauce, Salt, Pepper and corn starch.
  4. Add Quinoa into the bowl and knead them inside the bowl.
  5. Add Baby Bok Choy and shedded carrots.
  6. Add Roasted Black Sesame and mixed them well.
  7. Shape it into small Patty and have the frying pan ready.
  8. Pan fry 3 minutes on each side.
  9. Done and ready to serve.
