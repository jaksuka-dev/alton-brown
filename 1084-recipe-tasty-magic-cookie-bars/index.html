Recipe: Tasty Magic cookie bars

Magic cookie bars. Magic Cookie Bars – One of the easiest desserts you can make! Layered with butter graham cracker layer, chocolate chips, coconut and sweetened condensed milk. Magic cookie bars freeze beautifully when fully baked and can be eaten straight from the freezer for a chewier bar or thawed in the fridge for gift-giving.

Perfect for an easy dessert when you’re short on time! Made with a graham cracker buttery crust, layered with butterscotch chips. You can have Magic cookie bars using 9 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Magic cookie bars

  1. Prepare 1 stick of unsalted butter melted.
  2. You need 1 1/2 cups of gram cracker crumbs.
  3. You need of 1 can Sweetened condensed milk.
  4. You need of Pretzels broken up.
  5. You need of Carmels cut into fourths.
  6. Prepare of Semi sweet chocolate chips.
  7. Prepare of Peanut butter chips.
  8. Prepare of Salted peanuts or pecans.
  9. It’s of Sweetened coconut.

These Magic Cookie Bars with seven layers are a must-have Christmas cookie. A buttery graham cracker crust topped with chocolate and butterscotch are among other tasty ingredients. Cookie bars are on the menu and you’re absolutely going to love them. Even though the kids will adore these old-fashioned Magic Cookie Bars.

Magic cookie bars instructions

  1. Pre heat oven to 350 degrees and Melt butter in a 13×9 inch baking dish.
  2. Sprinkle on gram crackers crumbs evenly and pour on condensed milk evenly.
  3. Add Carmel pieces, Pretzels, chocolate chips, peanut butter chips, peanuts or pecans and coconut.
  4. Firmly press down with hands and bake for about 25 minutes or till coconut lightly browns.
  5. Cool completely before cutting into.

Each bite is filled with layers of cookie crumbs, chocolate chips, toasted coconut, and pecans.
