Dhaba style Egg Tadka step by step

  1. Pressure cook Whole moong Dal and potato for 30 minutes and keep aside..
  2. Heat oil in a large pan and add cumin seeds, Dry red chilli,chopped green chilies & bay leaves. When cumin seed starts cracking add onion and some salt and fry till onions becomes soften..
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and fry again. Add turmeric,red chili powder, coriander powder and fry it. Now add chopped tomatoes and cover it to cook the tomatoes very well. When the tomatoes are well cooked open the cover and stir fry the masala till oil separates from masala..
  4. Add the boiled dal and 1cup of water and bring the dal boil for 10 minutes. Now add the bhurji egg to the dal and mix it.
  5. After adding egg stir for some time and serve in a bowl and garnish with chopped coriander leaves and lemon and serve with hot roti or poori..
