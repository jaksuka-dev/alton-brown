Asian style Chicken step by step

  1. Begin by taking the 10 chicken thighs and putting them in a large mixing bowl..
  2. Next begin mixing thr marinade..
  3. Mix 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric with 2 tablespoons of chicken seasoning and 1/4 tablespoon of curry powder in a bowl..
  4. Using a knife peel the ginger root and mince it until the bits are barely visitble and form a mass..
  5. Mix the sesame oil with the ginger and add it to the spices. Mix everything into a rub and spread onto the chicken..
  6. Bake for 45 minutes to an hour at 350°F. Serve with sweet chily sauce.
