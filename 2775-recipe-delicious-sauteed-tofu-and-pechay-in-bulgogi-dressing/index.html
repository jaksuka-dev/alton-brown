Ingredients of Sautéed Tofu and Pechay in Bulgogi Dressing

  1. It’s 200 g of Pechay, Bok Choy or similar.
  2. Prepare 1-2 of small blocks fried tofu, diced.
  3. It’s 3 of tomatoes, sliced thin.
  4. You need 1/2 of garlic head, sliced lengthwise.
  5. Prepare 4-5 Tbsp of Bulgogi Marinade (Korean BBQ sauce).
  6. Prepare 2 Tbsp of Butter.
  7. Prepare of Cooking oil.
  8. It’s to taste of Salt,.
  9. It’s to taste of Pepper,.

Lightly coat the bottom of a pan with olive oil and heat on medium-high. Drain the marinade out of the bowl and place the tofu cubes in the heated pan. Sauté until the tofu cubes are lightly browned. Remove tofu from packaging and drain from packing liquid.

Sautéed Tofu and Pechay in Bulgogi Dressing step by step

  1. Prep your Pechay, wash and cut in bite-size pieces..
  2. In oil and butter, fry garlic until golden. Remove and set aside. Then in the same pan with the oil-butter, add the tomatoes. Simmer til soft and juicy. Add a pinch of salt..
  3. Add your pechay to the pan. Season with salt, pepper and 2 Tbsp Bulgogi marinade. Sauté and mix quicky..
  4. Drop in the fried tofu and add 1-2 tbsp more of Bulgogi marinade. Taste and adjust. Mix quickly and remove from heat..
  5. Plate and toss in the remaining Bulgogi marinade. Top with fried garlic and serve..
