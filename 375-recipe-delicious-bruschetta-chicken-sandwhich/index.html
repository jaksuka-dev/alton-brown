bruschetta chicken sandwhich step by step

  1. preheat oven to 400.
  2. boil chicken till almost cooked through.
  3. take boiled chicken out of pot and coat in asian zing sauce heavily.
  4. put chicken in oven and bake about 10 min.
  5. remove chicken when done and set aside.
  6. take loaf of bread and slice in half horizontally not cutting all the way through.
  7. take butter and spread on both halves of the bread.
  8. put bread on cookie sheet and stick in oven to warm the butter but not melt it.
  9. remove bread and spread pesto on both halves.
  10. take chicken and lay on one side of your bread.
  11. take bruschetta and evenly spread it over the chicken.
  12. take your mozzarella and spread over top of chicken.
  13. take and put your sandwhich back in the oven to melt the cheese.
  14. remove from oven when done, plate and enjoy!!.
