Spagetti with beef instructions

  1. Boil the spagetti for 15minutes,drain the water and rinse twice..
  2. Blend the fresh tomatoes and fresh pepper with ginger and garlic(keep 2 fresh tomatoes aside).
  3. Boil the paste till is dry,pour in vegetable oil and stir very well for about 10minutes..
  4. Cut the onions and pour inside,add salt,curry and seasoning..
  5. Boil the beef with salt,onion and maggi cubes with curry for 30minutes..
  6. Pour in the meat with the stock in the pot and allow it to boil for 10minutes.select the meat out and keep aside..
  7. Pour in the spagetti,stir very well and cover to boil till the water drys and spagetti soft..
  8. Bring it down,slice the fresh tomatoes and beans.
