Fried pita bread with chicken masala instructions

  1. Mix the yeast with 1 cup of water and allow to form.
  2. Place the flower in a bowl, add the flower, melted butter, baking powder, curry, and seasoning.
  3. Mix the yeast with the flour to form a soft dough.
  4. Cover and let it rest for 10 minimum.
  5. Roll out the dough in a floured surface, and cut into desirable size.
  6. Deep fry in oil, on a hot flame and drain in a paper towel to absorb excess oil.
  7. For the masala.
  8. Melt the butter in a pan.
  9. Add onions, garlic and habanero peppers and bay leaf.
  10. Sauté for 2 minutes.
  11. Add the chicken pieces, curry, gram masala, salt and seasoning to taste.
  12. Keep stir frying, for 3 more minutes.
  13. Add the green bell peppers and chicken stock,.
  14. Cook for a minute then turn the heat off and serve with the pita bread.
