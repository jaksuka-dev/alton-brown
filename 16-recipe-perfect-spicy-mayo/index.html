Creamy, tangy, spicy, and garlic-y, this incredible supersauce that adorns many sushi rolls can be achieved with two simple cupboard staples—sriracha and mayonnaise. You know the spicy mayo you get in sushi restaurants and grocery stores? To make Spicy Mayo at home, it is as simple as mixing mayonnaise and sriracha sauce together. You can cook Spicy Mayo using 5 ingredients and 3 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Spicy Mayo

  1. It’s 1 cup of mayonnaise.
  2. It’s 1 tsp of Himalayan Salt.
  3. It’s 1 tsp of Fresh Ground Pepper.
  4. Prepare 1/2 of lemon, juices and zested.
  5. It’s to taste of Sriracha Hot Sauce.

I highly recommend using Japanese mayonnaise for a richer flavor. The most popular brand you can. This spicy mayo recipe is so easy and quick to make, you never have to buy store-bought mayonnaise again. Try this spicy mayo sauce if you like that delicious restaurant-style spicy mayo dipping sauce!

Spicy Mayo step by step

  1. In a small mixing bowl, combine all the ingredients and mix well..
  2. Shake the sriracha sauce well for thickness when adding. Add as much and taste often to determine your desire without overpowering the mayo and lemon juice. Balance is key! Cover and refrigerate. (Lasts about four to five days for best quality.).
  3. Add to your favorite sandwiches or burgers!.
