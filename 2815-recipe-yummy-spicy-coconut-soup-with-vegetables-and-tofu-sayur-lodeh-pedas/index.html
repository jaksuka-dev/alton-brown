Ingredients of Spicy Coconut Soup with Vegetables and Tofu (Sayur Lodeh Pedas)

  1. You need 200 gr of tempeh (soy bean cake).
  2. You need 2 of chokos.
  3. Prepare 200 gr of white tofu.
  4. You need 1/2 of red onion.
  5. You need 3 of garlic cloves.
  6. It’s 3 of long red chilies.
  7. You need 3 of jalapenos (optional).
  8. It’s 2 cups of coconut milk.
  9. You need 1 cm of galangal.
  10. It’s 1 of bayleaf.
  11. Prepare 2 cups of water.
  12. You need 1/2 tsp of salt.
  13. It’s 1/2 tsp of pepper.
  14. It’s 1/2 tsp of sugar.
  15. It’s 2 tbsp of cooking oil.

Spicy Coconut Soup with Vegetables and Tofu (Sayur Lodeh Pedas) instructions

  1. Cut up tempeh, tofu, and chokos, put aside..
  2. Grind red onion, garlics, and long red chilies, put aside..
  3. Pre-heat cooking oil in a pot, cook ground ingredients until a little bit dry, add salt, sugar, pepper,galangal, jalapenos, and bayleaf, add water, mix well..
  4. Add chokos, tempeh, and tofu, wait until they are cooked properly..
  5. Add coconut milk, stir it until the soup boils properly..
  6. Serve it with steam rice, perfect to consume on cold days..
