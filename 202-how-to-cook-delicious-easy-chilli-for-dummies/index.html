Ingredients of Easy chilli for dummies

  1. Prepare 1 can of tomato juice.
  2. It’s 1 lb of ground chuck.
  3. Prepare 1 can of diced tomatoes.
  4. You need 2/3 cup of spagetti.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of sriracha sauce (optional).
  6. Prepare 1 can of chilli beans.
  7. It’s 1 packages of chilli seasoning.
  8. It’s 1/2 cup of chopped onion.

Easy chilli for dummies instructions

  1. Combine ground chuck and onions in a skillet until brown. Drain..
  2. Boil spaghetti until done..
  3. Combine tomato juice, chilli seasoning, chilli beans, diced tomatoes, spaghetti and ground chuck with onions in a large pot..
  4. Simmer on medium heat for 30 min..
  5. For extra spice add sriracha sauce or your favorite hot sauce..
  6. Enjoy!.
