Recipe: Yummy Delicious Breakfast Sandwhich!

  • Alton Brown
  • Tuesday, Jun 23, 2020

Delicious Breakfast Sandwhich!.

You can cook Delicious Breakfast Sandwhich! using 15 ingredients and 12 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Delicious Breakfast Sandwhich!

  1. You need 2 tbsp of butter.
  2. Prepare 2 of eggs.
  3. Prepare 1 dash of of milk.
  4. You need 2 tsp of chopped onion.
  5. Prepare 1 dash of salt, pepper, garlic salt.
  6. It’s 1 oz of sliced, sauteed mushrooms (optional).
  7. You need 2 slice of tomatoes.
  8. Prepare 2 slice of lettuce.
  9. Prepare 1 pinch of chopped parsley.
  10. It’s 1 slice of swiss cheese.
  11. It’s 1 of mayo (optional).
  12. You need 3 slice of cooked bacon.
  13. You need 1 bunch of roughly chopped spinach.
  14. You need 2 slice of white bread (or bagel , halved).
  15. It’s 4 of thinl slices of avacado (option).

Delicious Breakfast Sandwhich! step by step

  1. in a medium skillet melt the butter until it comes to a foam.
  2. add the finely chopped onion to the butter and cook until translucent/tender (if your using mushrooms too, saute them with the onions).
  3. meanwhile, whisk the eggs, milk, and seasonings in a bowl.
  4. pour the egg mix in skillet with cooked onions. reduce to medium-low heat, do not scramble eggs, let it cook a bottom layer.
  5. when the eggs form a cooked layer, gently push the edges in and tilt the pan so that the uncooked egg fills that area. do this around all the edges.
  6. after most of the excess egg has been sifted and cooked, flip the cooked egg (frittata) without breaking as much as possible..
  7. place the sliced cheese over the egg and let it cook for 2-3 minutes. top with chopped parsely.
  8. toast the slices of bread.
  9. spread mayo on insides of both slices of bread (optional).
  10. transfer frittata (cooked egg) to the bread..
  11. top with spinach, cooked bacon, tomato, and lettuce.
  12. close sandwhich with 2nd slice of bread, cut sandwhich in half and enjoy!.
