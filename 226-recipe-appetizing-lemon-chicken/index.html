Recipe: Appetizing Lemon chicken

  • Alton Brown
  • Saturday, May 16, 2020

Lemon chicken. Lemon chicken is the name of several dishes found in cuisines around the world which include chicken and lemon. In Canadian Chinese cuisine it usually consists of pieces of chicken meat that are sautéed or deep-fried and coated with a thick, sweet lemon-flavored sauce. With this recipe you get perfectly tender, golden brown pan seared chicken breasts that are coated in that quick lemon pan sauce.

Chicken thighs roasted with figs and lemon slices in a lemon, vinegar and brown sugar glaze. Seasoned, browned chicken breasts slow cooked with lemon juice, garlic, and chicken bouillon. This Lemon Chicken is an easy, crispy and delicious dish that’s sweet and savory, balanced with tangy flavor. You can cook Lemon chicken using 11 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Lemon chicken

  1. Prepare of About 2cups flour.
  2. It’s of Chicken 3 large boneless breast of chicken.
  3. It’s of Vegetable oil.
  4. You need of Butter.
  5. It’s of Chicken broth.
  6. It’s of Lemon juice.
  7. Prepare of Black pepper/ salt.
  8. It’s of Scallions.
  9. You need of Fresh parsley.
  10. You need of Garlic.
  11. Prepare of Olive oil.

Give chicken a zing with lemon juice and zest. Whether it’s sticky lemon chicken or a simple roast, this humble citrus fruit takes it to the next level. This easy lemon chicken recipe is one of our most popular main dish chicken recipes. With simple flavor additions that don’t overpower the dish, the natural flavor of the chicken is allowed to shine.

Lemon chicken step by step

  1. Cut chicken breast into chucks..about 2inches..coat with flour..you can put flour in a zip lock and add chicken chucks a few at a time shake bag to coat pieces..get vegetable oil hot in a pan..fry your chicken pieces a few at a time. Till golden brown..set aside on paper towels to absorb oil..in a large sauce pan saute in olive oil about 6 or7 large cloves of garlic cut and chopped.
  2. Then add 2 sticks of salted butter.
  3. Let melt..do not burn keep ur flame low..slowly add chicken chucks..saute for a few minutes get nice and hot..add chopped fresh parsley and chopped scallions only the bottoms not the stems…when ur chicken has cooked for about 10 minutes add enough chicken broth to cover the chicken..then add about 1/2cup lemon juice..cook on high heat for about 10 more minutes. Taste add salt and pepper.more lemon if u think needs it shut ur flame, put chopped scallion stems on top( and fresh italian parsley.
  4. Let stand for 15 min before serving.
  5. Add a little grated cheese on top(if u want)pecorino romano…or parmagiano.
  6. Serve over rice or pasta..or on its own…

Place chicken breasts in a gallon-size resealable plastic bag. Pour in lemon juice, olive oil, lemon pepper, basil, oregano, and salt. Shake it a bit so all the chicken get covered.
