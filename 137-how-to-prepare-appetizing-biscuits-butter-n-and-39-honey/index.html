Ingredients of Biscuits Butter n' Honey

  1. Prepare 500 gr of plain flour.
  2. It’s 250 of soft butter (Temperature room at least 1 hour).
  3. It’s 3/4 of honey.
  4. You need 1 tsp of Vanilla extract.

Biscuits Butter n' Honey step by step

  1. Sift the flour to a bowl.. make a small hole in the middle add butter, honey and vanilla extract combine together until make a dought and not stick in your hand.
  2. Give a rest in the fridge for at least 10 minute while you preheat the oven 160°C.
  3. Cut into half the dough and roll over until thick 1 cm.. Cut using the shape cutter or any shape you want.. I have a circle and put in the tray with fork to make a hole ready to bake…
  4. Bake for 10 / 12 minutes until golden brown…
  5. Put in the rack leave it until cool down.. After cool store in the jar.. And ready for accompany your cup of tea or even coffee!!.
