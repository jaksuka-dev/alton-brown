Ingredients of Double PB & Milk Chocolate Cookies

  3. Prepare 1 cup of softened butter.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of creamy peanut butter.
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup of sugar.
  6. You need 1/2 cup of brown sugar.
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract.
  8. It’s 1 of egg.
  9. It’s 1-2/3 cups of milk chocolate peanut butter morsels.

Double PB & Milk Chocolate Cookies instructions

  1. Preheat oven 375° F.
  2. In a small bowl mix flour and baking soda.
  3. In a large bowl combine butter, peanut butter, sugars and vanilla till light and creamy.
  4. Mix in egg..
  5. Mix in flour a little at a time.
  6. Stir in morsels.
  7. Drop on to a increased Baking sheet tablespoon at a time.
  8. Bake for 8-10 min or until edges are set but centers are still soft..cool completely.
  9. Makes about 3 doz..
