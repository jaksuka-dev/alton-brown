Ingredients of Best Homemade Mayonnaise

  1. Prepare 2 of large egg yolks.
  2. Prepare 2 tsp of white vinegar.
  3. It’s 1/8 tsp of salt.
  4. Prepare 2/3 C of vegetable oil.

Homemade mayonnaise is easy to make and tastes amazing. A few drops at a time is best until you notice the mayonnaise starting to thicken. The trick for homemade mayonnaise that keeps its emulsion: whisk in a few drops of water. For a good workout and a great emulsion, try making mayonnaise by hand with a whisk.

Best Homemade Mayonnaise instructions

  1. In a food processor, add the egg yolks, vinegar and salt, pulse a few times to combine.
  2. With the speed on high, slowly add the oil through the top vent…it will thicken as the oil is added.
  3. That’s all there is to it! The whole process takes about 3 minutes.
  4. For a Miracle Whip taste, substitute red wine vinegar for the white vinegar, add 1/2 tsp of mustard…that’s it!.
