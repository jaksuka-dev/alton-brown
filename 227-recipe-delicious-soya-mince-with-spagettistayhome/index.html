Ingredients of Soya mince with Spagetti#stayhome

  1. You need 1 of big bowl spagetti cooked.
  2. It’s 1 bowl of soya mince.
  3. It’s 1 of onoin.
  4. Prepare 3 of tomatoes pureed.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of ginger garlic paste.
  6. It’s to taste of Salt.
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp of turmeric powder.
  8. It’s 3/4 tsp of red chilli powder.
  9. It’s 1/2 tsp of black pepper.
  10. It’s 1/2 tsp of garam masala.
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup of spinach blanched chopped(optional).

Soya mince with Spagetti#stayhome instructions

  1. Soak the soya mince for 1 hr then drain in a sieve.In a pan add oil. Add chopped inion,cook till pink.
  2. Add the ginger garlic paste. Then add the tomato puree & all the spices & the spinach.
  3. Once cooked add the soya mince. Mix well.Cover & cook for 20 mins.
  4. Now add the cooked spagetti. Mix well. Cook for 5 mins &done.
  5. Now transfer in a plate, add grated cheese & other choice of toppings.
