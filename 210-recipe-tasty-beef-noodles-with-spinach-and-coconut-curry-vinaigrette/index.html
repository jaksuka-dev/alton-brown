beef noodles with spinach and coconut/curry vinaigrette step by step

  1. combine coconut milk,juice,soy sauce,vinegar,honey, whisk to combine.
  2. cook noodles as directed on package keep warm.
  3. heat oil in large saute pan season beef with salt and pepper sear in oil remove as done to a plate.
  4. add bok choy and spinach to skillet and cook until wilted add ginger garlic amd curry powder cook a min or two add coconut milk mixture cook about 5 min.
  5. add beef to heat through and serve over reserved noodles sprinkle with sesame seeds.
