Crustless Tofu Quiche step by step

  1. Preheat your oven 360°/180°.
  2. Rinse and drain the water from the package of tofu. Tear the tofu with hands and place in a mixing bowl..
  3. Mush it with a whisk..
  4. Add eggs and combine well..
  5. Add chopped ham(I used spam and omitted salt this time), jalapeño, cheese, herbs and spices. Mix well..
  6. Put them in a baking dish and bake about 35mins..
  7. Serve warm!.
