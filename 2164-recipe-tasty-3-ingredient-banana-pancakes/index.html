Recipe: Tasty 3 Ingredient Banana Pancakes

  • Alton Brown
  • Wednesday, Jul 8, 2020

3 Ingredient Banana Pancakes. In a bowl, mash the banana with a fork. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Flourless banana pancakes are held together with eggs in this quick and easy recipe for pancakes that fits into the paleo diet too!

A few years ago I tried making flourless banana pancakes for the first time. Two-ingredient pancakes, which just happen to have no gluten, are one of the internet’s favorite things. Maybe you’ve made them yourself, with just banana and egg. You can have 3 Ingredient Banana Pancakes using 3 ingredients and 8 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of 3 Ingredient Banana Pancakes

  1. It’s 1 of Banana.
  2. Prepare 2 of Large Eggs.
  3. It’s 1 Cup of Any Kind of Flour.

These oat flour pancakes are perfect for breakfast or as a healthy dessert. What do you normally eat for breakfast? Recipe posted in: Breakfasts, Flex™ Meals & Snacks. Some mornings are just made for pancakes, right?

3 Ingredient Banana Pancakes instructions

  1. Mash the banana in a bowl with a fork. -If you have a food processer or blender(Individual blender like the magic bullet work best) you can use those to speed things up. Just add in banana and egg and slowly add the flour into the mixture.-.
  2. Add the eggs into to bowl and mix them in..
  3. Add little bits if flour at a time and mix it in until pancake consistently.-picture was taken after I made the first pancake so you should have more than that-.
  4. Turn on frying pan to between low and medium heat and butter the bottom of the pan. -you can use oil instead of butter but I find it tastes better with butter-.
  5. Pour pancake mix into pan. This recipe make 2-3 pancakes depending on how big or small you make them..
  6. Let cook for 2-4 min until the outside of the pancake starts to crisp underneath. Or until golden brown. Flip pancake. -These do not look like normal pancakes most of the time. They will be splotchy in their coloration.-.
  7. Let cook for another 1-3 min. And then take it out and serve..
  8. Eat up and Enjoy!.
