How to Cook Yummy Quick Easy Chicken Sandwhich

  • Alton Brown
  • Wednesday, Feb 19, 2020

Quick Easy Chicken Sandwhich.

You can cook Quick Easy Chicken Sandwhich using 6 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Quick Easy Chicken Sandwhich

  1. It’s 1 of Costco frozen Chicken Patties(recommended) but any chicken will do.
  2. It’s 1 of sourdough bread.
  3. It’s 1 of lettuce not shredded.
  4. You need 1 of mayonnaise.
  5. You need 1 of ketchup.
  6. You need 1 of salt/pepper *optional.

Quick Easy Chicken Sandwhich step by step

  1. Microwave chicken patties 2 minutes then flip and do another two.
  2. Toast the bread.
  3. Tear lettuce.
  4. Spread ketchup on one piece of bread and mayonnaise on the other.
  5. Put them in order of bread with mayonnaise, lettuce, chicken, bread with ketchup. Or any way you want :P.
  6. Taste it, add salt and or pepper if needed (I needed it).
