  4. Prepare of Salt and Pepper.
  5. Prepare 2 of medium tomatoes peeled, cored, and sliced roughly chopped.
  6. You need 1-2 Tbs of olive oil.
  7. Prepare 1/3 cup of diced shallots.
  8. Prepare 1-2 of garlic cloves minced.
  9. Prepare of Salt and pepper.
  10. Prepare Dash of or so of white wine.
  11. You need of Red pepper flakes.
  12. You need 1-2 oz. of Feta cheese crumbled.
  13. It’s of Freeze-dried mint leave flakes or fresh.
  14. It’s of Cooking, assembly, and baking.
  15. You need of Preheat the oven to 400f.
  16. Prepare of In a bowl, add a little olive oil, salt, pepper, a few dashes of oregano, and toss the shrimp in the seasoned oil to coat and set aside.
  17. You need of Heat a medium saute pan to medium and add 1-2 Tbs Olive Oil.
  18. It’s of Add shallots, garlic, and salt and pepper to taste, saute until soft, about five minutes.
  19. You need of Add the tomatoes, and red pepper flakes. Simmer, mashing the tomatoes a bit. Cover and cook down until thickened. Remove the lid and cook to a sauce.
  20. Prepare of Add a splash of white wine and simmer for a few minutes more.
  21. It’s of Transfer the tomatoes, shallots, and garlic onto individual ovenproof serving dishes and spread across the bottom.
  22. Prepare of Layer the shrimp across the tomatoes and crumble the feta over the shrimp.
  23. It’s of Put into the oven and cook about 10-15 minutes until bubbly.
