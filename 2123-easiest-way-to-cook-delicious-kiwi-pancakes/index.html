Ingredients of Kiwi Pancakes

  1. Prepare 2 of kiwi fruits.
  2. Prepare 1 of egg.
  3. It’s 1/2 cup of thick plain yogurt.
  4. You need 3 tbsp of sugar.
  5. It’s 3/4 cup of self-rising flour.

Kiwi Pancakes step by step

  1. Peel kiwi fruits. Puree one using a food processor. (You can also grate it or chop it finely) Slice another for decoration..
  2. Combine egg and yogurt. I like to use a whisk, but a fork would do just fine. Add sugar and mix well..
  3. Add the pureed kiwi and fold a few times..
  4. Add self-rising flour bit by bit, fold the batter a few times. Do not overmix..
  5. Wait for about 5 minutes until bubbles arise..
  6. Heat a nonstick pan. (low heat) Pour batter and wait until it bubbles. Flip and wait until it no longer sticks in a pan. Repeat the process..
  7. Serve with sliced kiwi..
