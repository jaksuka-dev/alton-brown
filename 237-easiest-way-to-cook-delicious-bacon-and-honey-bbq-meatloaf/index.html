Ingredients of Bacon and Honey BBQ Meatloaf

  1. Prepare 2 pounds of ground beef.
  2. You need 1 1/2 cups of bread crumbs.
  3. You need 1 cup of BBQ sauce.
  4. You need 1 tablespoon of honey.
  5. It’s 1 of small to medium onion (diced).
  6. It’s 6-8 strips of bacon.
  7. Prepare 2 of eggs.

Bacon and Honey BBQ Meatloaf instructions

  1. Preheat over to 350 degrees.
  2. In large bowl: mix bread crumbs, ground beef, eggs, BBQ sauce, honey and diced onion..
  3. Spray non-stick spray in a loaf pan. Put contents of bowl into the loaf pan and flatten out top of loaf. Place pan in larger pan if afraid of grease overflow..
  4. Place strips of bacon lengthwise on top of loaf until the loaf is covered..
  5. Place in oven for 45-55 minutes..
  6. Serve hot with favorite veggie on the side..
