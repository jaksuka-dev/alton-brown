How to make easy temari sushi(sushi ball) step by step

  1. For serves 2, cook 1.5 cups of rice, add 1.5 cups of water, cooking wine and conbu.
  2. If you don’t have sushi vinegar with you, make the easy sushi vinegar. Mix the rice vinegar, sugar and salt then cook over low heat until the sugar and salt melted..
  3. Add 1tbsp sushi vinegar into the rice and well mixed, cool the rice..
  4. Cut the sashimi thinly. Use the saran wrap, put the fish under the rice then twist it to make the sushi ball..
