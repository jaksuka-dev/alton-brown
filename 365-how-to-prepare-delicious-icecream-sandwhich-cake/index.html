Icecream sandwhich cake! step by step

  1. Get a square cake pan (use square so icecream sandwiches will fit better)..
  2. Layer bottom of pan with icecream sandwhich. You may need to cut sandwhich to fit. (I used chocolate/vanilla ones but any brand and flavor will work)..
  3. Spread whipped topping on top of sandwiches. Thawed whipped topping is easier to spread. (Any brand will work)..
  4. Put Oreos (name brand or off brand is fine) in ziplock baggy and start crushing them..
  5. Sprinkle crushed Oreos on top of whipped topping. Enjoy now or freeze until later!.
