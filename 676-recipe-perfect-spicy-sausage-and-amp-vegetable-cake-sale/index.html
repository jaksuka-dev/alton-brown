Recipe: Perfect Spicy Sausage & Vegetable Cake Salé

  • Alton Brown
  • Thursday, Apr 30, 2020

Spicy Sausage & Vegetable Cake Salé.

You can cook Spicy Sausage & Vegetable Cake Salé using 11 ingredients and 10 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Spicy Sausage & Vegetable Cake Salé

  1. You need 300 g (10.58 oz) of Pancake mix.
  2. It’s 200 ml (7.03 fl oz) of Milk.
  3. Prepare 2 of Eggs.
  4. You need 10 of Spicy sausages *finger size.
  5. It’s 100 g (3.52 oz) of Tomato.
  6. It’s 100 g (3.52 oz) of Broccoli.
  7. It’s 100 g (3.52 oz) of Sweet potato.
  8. Prepare 100 g (3.52 oz) of Kabocha squash.
  9. Prepare to taste of Truffle salt.
  10. You need to taste of Truffle powder.
  11. It’s to taste of Shredded cheese.

Spicy Sausage & Vegetable Cake Salé step by step

  1. Make the pancake mixture per instruction..
  2. Add a pinch of truffle salt & powder, if you have them or would like..
  3. Chop vegetables as you like or that need to be used up, and then put in a microwave safe bowl, wrap it, and heat at 600 W for 5 mins. to make the vegetables soften. *Vegetables which are soft without cooking, like tomatoes, do not have to heat up..
  4. Pre-heat the oven at 200℃ (392℉)..
  5. Pour 1/2 cake mixture in the baking tray, place vegetables, cover it with shredded cheese, and then put sausages on top of that..
  6. Pour the rest of the cake mixture on it..
  7. Cover the top with shredded cheese again..
  8. Bake at 200℃ (392℉) for 20 mins..
  9. Adjust the baking temperature or time. *Check the cake few times. If the cake is inflated and becomes light brown, it should be cooked okay..
  10. Slice and serve!.
