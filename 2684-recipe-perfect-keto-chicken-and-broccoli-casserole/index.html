Keto Chicken and Broccoli Casserole instructions

  1. Remove meat from chickens, dice and add to large mixing bowl.
  2. Steam broccoli, dice and add to bowl.
  3. Add all other ingredients except Cheddar to bowl and mix evenly.
  4. Pour into casserole dish and top with shredded cheddar.
  5. Bake at 375 for 30min.
  6. Allow 15min to cool before serving.
