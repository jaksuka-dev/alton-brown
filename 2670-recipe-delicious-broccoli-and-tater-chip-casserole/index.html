Broccoli and Tater Chip Casserole step by step

  2. Brown the beef and sausage with the onions and garlic in it. Season this with some seasoned salt and pepper..
  3. Steam the broccoli and season with seasoned salt. Add to a baking pan..
  4. Drain the meat. Add on top of the broccoli..
  5. Add crushed potato chips.
  6. Melt the butter and add the milk. Heat and add the shredded cheese when the cheese melts add the cornstarch to the water and mix..
