Sayur asem kacang tanah translate: sour soup veggie with peanuts step by step

  1. Pour water into large pan, bring to boil along with your herbs+tamarine paste and raw skinned peanuts.
  2. When the soup is boiled bring in the paste and corn chunks,season well with sugar,salt and stock. Taste it!.
  3. Bring in the melinjo and melinjo leaves, chinese squash,simmer a little bit then bring in the string beans. Stir. Cook well then put the heat off..
  4. Last step put the ground roasted peanuts stir until well combined..
