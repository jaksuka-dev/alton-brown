Ingredients of Italian parmesan chicken

  1. It’s 2 tbsp of flour.
  2. It’s 1 of egg, beaten.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of italian bread crumbs.
  4. You need 1/4 cup of graded parmesan cheese.
  5. It’s 4 of boneless chicken breasts (pounded 1/2 inch thick).
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp of melted butter.
  7. It’s 3 head of spagetti noodles.
  8. Prepare 1 of progresso italian herb sauce.

Italian parmesan chicken instructions

  1. place flour, egg and bread crumbs on separate plates.
  2. coat chicken with flour, dip on egg, then coat WELL with bread crumb mixture.
  3. in a large skillet, heat over medium heat. COOK chicken 8-10 min. TURNING ONCE until chicken is golden brown.
  4. boil noodles and add Italian herbs, salt, and pepper to the water.
  5. take chicken out of skillet and add cheese, THICK, and connection or micro wave for 45 sec.
  6. put noodles on plate, add sauce, add chicken, and enjoy.
