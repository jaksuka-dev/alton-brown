Japanese style mapo tofu step by step

  1. Mix together sauce ingredients.
  2. Mince garlic and ginger finely and sauté on medium until fragrant.
  3. Add meat.
  4. When meat is brown add sauce mixture and stir.
  5. Add tofu and stir until coated with sauce.
  6. Sprinkle chopped up scallions before turning off heat.
  7. Serve over rice.
