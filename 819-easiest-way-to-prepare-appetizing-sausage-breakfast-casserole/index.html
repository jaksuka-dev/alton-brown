Ingredients of Sausage breakfast casserole

  1. It’s 7 of eggs beaten.
  2. It’s 1/4 cup of milk.
  3. You need 1 of red pepper chopped (optional).
  4. You need 1 of onion chopped finely.
  5. Prepare 3 of medium or small potatoes diced finely.
  6. You need 1 bag of low fat cheddar cheese.
  7. Prepare 1 lb of loose sausage browned and drained.
  8. Prepare to taste of Salt and pepper.

Sausage breakfast casserole instructions

  1. Brown sausage, onion, potatoes, and peppers (and drain if needed).
  2. Wisk eggs, milk, and seasoning and half the bag of cheese.
  3. Place cooked sausage and veggies in greased casserole dish.
  4. Pour egg mixture over and then top with remaining cheese.
  5. Bake at 350 for 55 min. Or cover and bake next morning for fresh breakfast.
