Chiken chukka step by step

  2. Next step is marinating the chiken for that add these fried onions to chiken then add all these masala which I mentioned in ingredients marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. After 30 minutes we have to transfer these into a tawa and star to cook you are not supposed to add water as the water from chikn is sufficient.
