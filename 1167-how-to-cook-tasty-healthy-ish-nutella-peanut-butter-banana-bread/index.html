Ingredients of Healthy-ish Nutella Peanut Butter Banana Bread

  1. Prepare 3 of very ripe bananas.
  2. You need 2 of eggs.
  3. It’s 2 cups of rolled oats.
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup of maple syrup.
  5. It’s 3/4 cup of creamy peanut butter.
  6. It’s 1 tsp of baking soda.
  7. You need 1 teaspoon of vanilla.
  8. It’s 2 tablespoons of nutella.

Healthy-ish Nutella Peanut Butter Banana Bread instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Place all ingredients except Nutella in blender and blend until smooth.
  3. Grease 9 inch loaf pan and add batter.
  4. Bake for 30 min.
  5. Garnish with banana slices, drizzle with Nutella. Enjoy!.
