Ingredients of Dressing of Rye and Sausage

  1. You need 6 slices of Rye bread with seeds.
  2. You need 1/2 pound of smoked sausage.
  3. It’s 1 cup of sliced celery.
  4. Prepare 1 of large onion diced.
  5. It’s 1 pint of chicken broth.
  6. You need 2 of large egg.
  7. You need 1/2 teaspoon of ground sage.
  8. It’s 1/3 teaspoon of granulated garlic powder.
  9. You need 1 teaspoon of kosher salt.
  10. It’s 1/2 cup of Golden raisins.
  11. It’s 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil.

Dressing of Rye and Sausage instructions

  1. Preheat the oven 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Tear the rye bread into small chunks. Spread it out on a baking pan. Put it in the oven till dried out..
  3. Slice the sausage into pieces that are manageable..
  4. Add oil and sausage to the pan and fry with the raisins, onions, and celery..
  5. Mix the toasted rye bread, eggs, chicken broth, spices, and sausage mixture. Add to a baking pan..
  6. Bake in the oven for 40-45 minutes..
  7. Let rest 7 minutes and serve. I hope you enjoy!!.
