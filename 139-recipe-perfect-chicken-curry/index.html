Recipe: Perfect Chicken Curry

  • Alton Brown
  • Saturday, Jun 27, 2020

Chicken Curry.

You can have Chicken Curry using 14 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Chicken Curry

  1. You need 500 gm of Chiken.
  2. Prepare 250 gm of pyaj.
  3. Prepare 1 spoon of lahsun pest.
  4. You need 1 spoon of Adarak pest.
  5. Prepare 1 spoon of Haldi pawdar.
  6. Prepare 1 spoon of dhaniya pawdar.
  7. It’s 1 spoon of Lal mirch pawdar.
  8. It’s 2 of Daalchini.
  9. It’s 2-3 of tezpatta.
  10. Prepare 3-3 of Long & ilyacha.
  11. Prepare 1/2 spoon of khaskhas.
  12. It’s of Dhaniya Patti.
  13. It’s of Salt.
  14. Prepare of Oil.

Chicken Curry instructions

  1. Chiken ke tukdo ko achhe se dho le. fir ek kadahi me 3 spoon oil daal kar chikan ko daal de. fir namak daal kr chalye. 10 minut tak chalye fir plate me nikal le, long, ilaycha, Sonf, daalchini or 2 tez ptte ko tave me daal kr bhun le. ab in msalo ko mixi ke jaar me daal de..or 250 gm. pyaj ko bhi daal kar graind kar le..
  2. Ab kadahi me oil daal kar 1 tezpata daal de fir graind kiye masalo ko daal de or lahsun pest or adarak pest ko bhi daal de….
  3. Ab daniya, Haldi or Mirch pawdar daal deijiye. achhe se bunte rahiye.. ab salt bhi daal dijiye..fir chalte rahiye…
  4. Ab jab masalo ki khushbu ane lage or kadahi tel chodne lage tab masala rady.
  5. Ab isme chiken jo dal kar chalaye 5 minut fir paani daal kar chaye. or thoda salt daal kr. dhak dijiye 5 minut. fir dhaniya patti daal dijiye.. chiken carry Rady…
