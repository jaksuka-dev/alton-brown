Recipe: Delicious Butter Cookies 🧈🍪

  • Wednesday, Aug 26, 2020

Butter Cookies 🧈🍪. These butter cookies have many different names (Danish butter cookies/melting moments/Pastisetas and more). It doesn’t matter how you call these cookies. Butter cookies (or butter biscuits), known as Brysselkex, Sablés, and Danish biscuits, are unleavened cookies consisting of butter, flour, and sugar.

I too added just a little more flour and a little more Peanut Butter. The peanut butter cookie recipe that most resembles modern peanut butter cookies, and introduced the now familiar cross-hatch pattern, first appeared in Ruth Wakefield’s Toll House What’s in peanut butter cookies? Just peanut butter, flour, butter, sugar, egg, salt, baking soda and baking powder. You can have Butter Cookies 🧈🍪 using 6 ingredients and 11 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Butter Cookies 🧈🍪

  1. It’s 120 g of Golden Churn Unsalted Butter (软膏状）.
  2. Prepare Pinch of salt.
  3. You need 35 g of coconut palm sugar.
  4. Prepare 1 g of vanilla essence.
  5. It’s 1 of egg yolk.
  6. You need 130 g of cake flour / low protein flour.

Butter cookies—also known as Dutch biscuits—are delicious, crispy cookies that are quick and easy to make. Even better, the recipe only uses pantry staples! Use a cookie press to turn the dough into beautiful shapes or roll the dough into a log and cut classic circles. These classic butter cookies from Delish.com are great for any occasion.

Butter Cookies 🧈🍪 step by step

  1. Mixer on Speed 1 for the entire process.
  2. Mix in butter & salt | medium speed till colour is slightly lighten (do not over mix).
  3. Mix in sugar by 2 batches (拌匀就可以了）.
  4. Use a spatula to clean the side.
  5. Mix in egg yolk & vanilla essence.
  6. Mix, clean the side, mix again. (看不见蛋黄）.
  7. Using cutting motion lightly mix in flour by 2 batches (do not over mix).
  8. Put mixture into pipping bag & pipe out (挤出曲奇的时候要挤高一点，纹路比较明显）.
  9. 150° 25mins (must look after it time to time).
