English Muffin Hamburger step by step

  1. Cut the English muffin in half and spread margarine on each side..
  2. If you have a muffin mold, coat the sides with shortening..
  3. Cook the muffin, hamburger steak, and egg in a frying pan. Season the sunny side up with salt and pepper..
  4. Fry only the buttered sides of the muffin. When lightly browned, transfer to a plate..
  5. When everything is cooked, arrange as you like and enjoy!.
