Honey baked Ham step by step

  2. Rub ham down with mustard & drizzle with honey & then pat down with dark brown sugar.
  3. Open can of pineapples pour out half the juice into the pan with the ham.
  4. Place the rings of pineapples all over the ham with a toothpick now grab a cherry pull toothpick out & place back in to secure both cherry & pineapple.
  5. Wrap tightly with aluminum foil & place in a preheated oven of 350 to 400 degrees for an hour & a 1/2.

  7. Now add more honey & pack with the light brown sugar now re-wrap lightly this time w/ foil & place back in oven for another hour.
