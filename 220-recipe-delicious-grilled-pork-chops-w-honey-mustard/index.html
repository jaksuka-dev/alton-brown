Recipe: Delicious Grilled Pork Chops (w/ honey-mustard)

  • Alton Brown
  • Wednesday, Apr 29, 2020

Grilled Pork Chops (w/ honey-mustard).

You can have Grilled Pork Chops (w/ honey-mustard) using 7 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Grilled Pork Chops (w/ honey-mustard)

  1. Prepare 4 of pork chops (thick cut).
  2. It’s Dash of salt, pepper, paprika, & thyme.
  3. It’s 2 tbs of fresh rosemary (chopped very fine).
  4. You need of Sauce:.
  5. You need 3 tbs of honey.
  6. Prepare 3 tbs of brown mustard.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of dried thyme.

Grilled Pork Chops (w/ honey-mustard) step by step

  1. Season the thick-cut chops on one side (include the rosemary) and refrigerate for 5 minutes..
  2. Fire up the grill. Reach a temperature of medium – high to cook..
  3. For the sauce, mix the honey, mustard, and thyme in a small cup. Set aside by the grill..
  4. Place the chops on the grill and sear for 3 minutes on one side. Turn them over and sear for another 3 minutes..
  5. Flip the chops over again, this time on a different angle on the grill. Add the sauce (with a spoon or brush) to the top of the chops and cook for 2 minutes over medium heat..
  6. Turn the chops over, add more sauce, and finish cooking for another 2 minutes at a medium – low heat..
  7. Plate the chops on a clean serving plate and allow them to rest for a few minutes before serving..
