How to Prepare Yummy Instant Pot Honey Chicken

  • Alton Brown
  • Saturday, Jun 6, 2020

Instant Pot Honey Chicken.

You can cook Instant Pot Honey Chicken using 12 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Instant Pot Honey Chicken

  1. It’s 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
  2. Prepare 4 of boneless chicken breasts diced.
  3. Prepare 1 tsp of salt.
  4. Prepare 2 tablespoons of cornstarch.
  5. It’s 2 tablespoons of water.
  6. Prepare of Honey Sauce.
  7. It’s 1/2 cup of soy sauce.
  8. You need 1/2 cup of honey.
  9. It’s 1/4 cup of ketchup.
  10. You need 3 cloves of garlic minced.
  11. Prepare 2 tsp of rice wine vinegar.
  12. You need 2 tsp of sesame oil.

Instant Pot Honey Chicken step by step

  1. Turn instant pot to sauté. Add olive oil and chicken. Cook until chicken is white..
  2. Whisk together ingredients of the honey sauce in a bowl..
  3. Pour sauce into the pot covering the chicken..
  4. Set on manual for 4 minutes. Quick release..
  5. Mix cornstarch and water and pour mixture into the pot stirring until thickened..
  6. Serve over rice. Add green onions to your liking..
