Ingredients of Mixed veg multigrain chilla sandwitch

  1. It’s 1/2 cup of aata,.
  2. You need 2 tsp each of gram flour,maize flour, jowar flour,millet flour.
  3. It’s 1 cup of chopped vegetables(carrot beans peas raddish,palak,gobhi).
  4. You need as needed of all spices.
  5. It’s as needed of oil for frying.
  6. It’s 100 gm of paneer.

So, catch this quick & easy mixed veg sandwich recipe which helps you up for nutrient We are using this to the maximum benefit by making a mixed veg sandwich. Veggies in the sandwich like Bell peppers, onions, tomatoes, corn are rich in vitamin. Multi-grain Chilla is an easy breakfast recipe made with wheat flour, gram flour and black millet flours. Here’s an instant, crispy and perfect filling recipe to accompany your tea or for a quick breakfast on a lazy weekend morning.

Mixed veg multigrain chilla sandwitch step by step

  1. Mix all veggies and all type of flour in mixing bowl add all spices according to taste.add water and make batter.
  2. Take tawa.pour few drops of oil and 2 spoons of batter make chilla or use cookie cutter and pour half spoon of batter..
  3. Cut paneer also of same shape,.
  4. Place paneer in center and seal with toothpick. ready to serve..
