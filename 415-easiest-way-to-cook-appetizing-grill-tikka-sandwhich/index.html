Easiest Way to Cook Appetizing Grill Tikka sandwhich

  • Alton Brown
  • Tuesday, Jul 7, 2020

Grill Tikka sandwhich.

You can cook Grill Tikka sandwhich using 17 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Grill Tikka sandwhich

  1. Prepare 1/2 kg of chicken boneless (small pieces).
  2. Prepare 1 teaspoon of Ginger garlic paste.
  3. Prepare 3 tablespoon of Thick yogurt.
  4. Prepare 1 tablespoon of Lemon juice or vinegar.
  5. Prepare 2 tablespoon of Chicken tikka masala.
  6. It’s 2 tablespoon of Ketchup.
  7. It’s 1/2 tablespoon of Mustard paste.
  8. You need 1/4 tsp of salt or to taste.
  9. You need slices of Bread (1 sandwhich required 3 slices).
  10. Prepare 4 of eggs.
  11. You need 1 of large tomato (sliced).
  12. You need 1 of cucumber (sliced).
  13. You need 1/2 cup of Mayonnaise.
  14. It’s 4 tablespoon of Tomato ketcup.
  15. You need 5 tablespoon of Chilli garlic.
  16. You need 1/2 teaspoon of honey.
  17. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon of vinegar.

Grill Tikka sandwhich step by step

  1. In a large bowl add chicken, salt, tikka masala, mustard paste,lemon juice, ginger garlic paste,tomato ketchup then add yogurt and mix well then merinate for 20 minutes.
  2. Now prepared dynamite sauce. In a bowl add mayonnaise, chilli garlic sauce, tomato ketchup,honey and vinegar and whisk well. Dynamite sauce is ready!.
  3. Now take a fry pan then add oil for 3 table spoon then add chicken in low medium flame for cook max 10 min.
  4. Now grill the bread slices on a grill pan.
  5. Now make fry egg.
  6. Take a bread slice and spread the mayonnaise then add tikka chicken and dynamite sauce now take second slice spread mayonnaise then add vegetables (tomato cucumber) then add egg and dynamite sauce and cover with 3rd bread slice..
  7. Your tasty chicken tikka sandwich is ready!!.
