Ingredients of Peanut bhindi chutney

  10. It’s of Turmeric Powder.

Peanut bhindi chutney instructions

  1. Wash and cut the bhendi into vertically into 4 slices lengthwise..
  2. In a blender add peanuts, cumin seeds, salt, turmeric, red chilli powder, garlic pods and make fine powder..
  3. Heat oil in a kadhai and add cumin seeds. Allow it to crackle and add chopped onions. Saute the onion till they turn light golden..
  4. Add the bhindi into it and cook it. Mix it when it starts to become tender and soft. Now add the prepared powder in it and saute for 2-3 minutes. Add lemon juice according to taste and serve..
