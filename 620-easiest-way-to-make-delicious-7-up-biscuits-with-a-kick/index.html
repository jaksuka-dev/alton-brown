Easiest Way to Make Delicious 7-Up Biscuits With A KICK!

  • Alton Brown
  • Sunday, Jul 26, 2020

7-Up Biscuits With A KICK!.

You can have 7-Up Biscuits With A KICK! using 7 ingredients and 8 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of 7-Up Biscuits With A KICK!

  1. You need 2 cup of Bisquick baking mix.
  2. It’s 1/2 cup of Sour cream.
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup of 7-up (I actually use diet ginger ale).
  4. It’s 1 cup of Extra sharp cheddar cheese – shredded.
  5. You need 1 cup of Bacon bits – home made or brand of your choice (I used Hormel).
  6. Prepare 2 of Finely chopped jalapeño peppers – I used fresh – but I imagine red pepper flakes would work as well.
  7. You need 4 tbsp of Butter – divided in half.

7-Up Biscuits With A KICK! instructions

  1. Pre heat oven to 400°F. Melt 2 TBL BUTTER IN THE BOTTOM OF THE PAN.
  2. Blend the sour cream into the Bisquick.
  3. Add the soda & mix to incorporate.
  4. Add the cheddar, bacon bits, & chopped jalapeños until incorporated.
  5. Place on baking sheet & dot the top of each biscuit with butter. I baked my biscuits for 20 minutes – your mileage may vary depending on oven true temperature.
  6. When finished, remove from oven & allow to rest before removing from pan.
  7. Only thing left to do is plate a bisquit.
  8. I like mine with a bit of organic raw honey.
