Easiest Way to Prepare Delicious Sesame Honey Chicken

  • Alton Brown
  • Monday, Aug 10, 2020

Sesame Honey Chicken.

You can have Sesame Honey Chicken using 22 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Sesame Honey Chicken

  1. Prepare of Chicken———————-.
  2. It’s 4-1/4 pound of chicken breast skin on bone in.
  3. You need 1 pound of broccoli.
  4. You need 1/4 cup of honey.
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon of ground ginger.
  6. Prepare 2 teaspoons of kosher salt.
  7. You need 1 teaspoon of sherry vinegar.
  8. It’s 1 teaspoon of ground white pepper.
  9. You need 1 teaspoon of granulated garlic powder.
  10. You need 1/2 cup of Shaoxing wine.
  11. Prepare 1/4 cup of coconut aminos.
  12. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of sesame seed oil.
  13. It’s 1-1/2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil.
  14. Prepare of Fried “rice”———————.
  15. It’s 12 ounces of cauliflower that’s been made riced.
  16. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of kosher salt.
  17. Prepare 2 tablespoons of coconut aminos.
  18. You need 1/2 of large onion diced.
  19. It’s 1 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil.
  20. Prepare 3 of large eggs.
  21. You need of Garnish———————.
  22. You need 1/4 cup of sesame seeds.

Sesame Honey Chicken instructions

  1. Beat the egg and fry done but no color. Set aside. Drain the cauliflower..
  2. Fry the onion in a very hot wok or skillet. Add the salt, aminos, and cauliflower and cook the liquids out..
  3. Add the eggs in. Stirfry the egg into the riced cauliflower..
  4. Remove the skin from the breast and fry the skins crispy. Set aside..
  5. Remove the chicken from the bones and cut into bitesized pieces. Use the bone to make a broth for another time. Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil, heat and add the cut up chicken. Add coconut aminos simmer10 minutes. Add the spices, sherry, and sesame seed oil. When the chicken is done add the broccoli. When all ingredients except the honey and sesame seeds are in cover and simmer till the broccoli is tender. Add the honey and reduce the liquids.
  6. Toast the sesame seeds. When done serve with toasted sesame seeds..
