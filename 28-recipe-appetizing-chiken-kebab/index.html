🥘Chiken Kebab🥘 step by step

  1. Place all of the ingredients into a food processor. The onion, green chillies, garam flour, garam masala, dry whole coriander, white cumin seeds, ginger-garlic paste, red chilli powder and salt..
  2. Blitz until all the ingredients resemble a coarsely paste. Don’t add any water during this process. Scrape down the sides using a spatula..
  3. Now add the chiken mince with egg, fresh coriander leaves and red chilli flakes and keep blitzing 3 to 4 time or just everything combined. Marinate the chicken mince 3 to 4 hours in a refrigerator..
  4. Make kebabs and keep a side. Heat oil in a non-stick pan, and place the kebabs and cook on medium flame till gets a golden colour all around. Place the kebabs on a paper towel..
