Ingredients of Chiken_corn_soup

  1. Prepare of Chicken boneless ½ kg * Chicken bones (chopped) ½ kg * Sw.
  2. You need of Hot chilli sauce 1 cup * Soya sauce 1 cup * White vinegar.
  3. Prepare of Green chilies 5 – 6 * Eggs 3 – 4 * Garlic cloves 4 – 5.
  4. It’s of Chinese salt ½ tsp * Corn flour 3 – 4 tbsp * Black pepper.
  5. Prepare of required (crushed).
  6. It’s to taste of Salt.

Chinese chicken corn soup is delicious and nutritious. It contains chicken meat, egg and tasty corn. With a slight tang to its taste, this soup makes a nice starter for a Chinese-themed meal or an. Sweet corn chicken soup is a classic soup among Indo Chinese recipes.

Chiken_corn_soup instructions

  1. 1Add chicken bones, garlic cloves and water as required in a pan..
  2. Cook to make chicken stock and strain..
  3. In the prepared stock, add sweet corn, boneless chicken and cook for 10 – 15 minutes.
  4. Then add crushed black pepper, salt, and Chinese salt..
  5. Now add beaten eggs while string with a spoon continuously..
  6. When the soup starts to boil, add corn flour mixed in water and stir continuously until the soup thickens..
  7. Pour into a large soup bowl and garnish with shredded chicken.
  8. Serve soup along soya sauce, chilli sauce, white vinegar, and boil egg slice….

An easy soup recipe made with cream style corn and shredded chicken is very flavorful. Chicken Corn Soup is a delicious and nutritional starter to have before meal. Chicken & Corn Soup has always been my number one pick at my local Chinese restaurant – and this recipe tastes just like that! Shredded chicken mixed through a creamy corn soup.
