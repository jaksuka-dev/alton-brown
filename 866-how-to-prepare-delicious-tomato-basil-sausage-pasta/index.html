Ingredients of Tomato Basil Sausage Pasta

  1. It’s 3 tbsp of butter.
  2. It’s 1/2 lb of sausage.
  3. Prepare 1/2 of onion minced.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of salt.
  5. It’s 1 tsp of pepper.
  6. Prepare 2 cups of marinara sauce.
  7. It’s 1 cup of milk.
  8. It’s 1/2 cup of basil.
  9. It’s 1/2 lb of cooked spaghetti.

Tomato Basil Sausage Pasta step by step

  1. Melt butter in medium size pan or wok then add sausage and cook for 4 minutes.
  2. Add minced onion and cook for 2 minutes then add salt and pepper.
  3. Add marinara sauce and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add milk and cook for 2 more minutes.
  5. Add basil and cook for 1 minutes.
  6. Add cooked spaghetti noodles and cook for 5 more minutes.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy.
