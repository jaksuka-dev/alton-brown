Olan white pumkin curry instructions

  1. Peel and cut the pumkin.
  2. Boil the pumkin with green chilli.
  3. Add the coconut milk, salt to it and boil it for 5-7 min.
  4. Garnish it with coconut oil and  curry leaves.
