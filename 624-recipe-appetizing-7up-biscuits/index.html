Recipe: Appetizing 7up biscuits

  • Alton Brown
  • Friday, Feb 21, 2020

7up biscuits. The dough will be very soft, don’t worry. They only have three ingredients – THE BEST biscuits EVER! We make these at least twice a week.

These biscuits are so quick and easy, and so fluffy and delicious at the same time. The dough will be very soft. They are also made with Bisquick, which is a premixed baking mix that has flour, baking soda. You can cook 7up biscuits using 6 ingredients and 1 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of 7up biscuits

  1. You need of killers 7 up biscuits(extra fluffy&soft).
  2. You need 2 cup of Bisquick.
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup of sour cream.
  4. It’s 1/2 cup of 7up soda….or sprite.
  5. Prepare pinch of of kosher salt or ground seasalt.
  6. You need 1/2 stick of of real salted butter melted.

These biscuits will work with a lot of different kinds of soda! The lemon lime drink is a not so secret ingredient to biscuit perfection! There is a bonus baking mix recipe for those. Flour your cutter each time before cutting your biscuits.

7up biscuits step by step

  1. Stir sour cream into the Bisquick slowly add 7up mix until completely mix together …melt butter place in pan coat it. .. then scoop. .. the dough in big/med spoon fulls on top of butter bake for 14 mins OR until golden brown… (note:it’s ok if they touch. Each other. .. after just rub more butter and get ready for some fluffy biscuits.
