Funkyfied Chicken n Biscuits instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375. Cook and dice the chicken and bacon.
  2. Mix Carrots, Drained Green Beans, Cream of Celery, Cream of Chicken Soup in a large bowl and set aside..
  3. Cook Penne Noodles, halfway add Chopped Celery. Drain and add to soup mixture..
  4. Pour into Large Casserole Dish and place Crescent Rolls on top. Bake for 20 min or until Crescents are golden brown. Pull out, top with Cheese and Bacon. Place back in oven until cheese is melted..
  5. Enjoy and let us see that snap!.
