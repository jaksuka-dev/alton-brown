Ingredients of Salisbury Steak

  1. It’s 1 lb of ground beef.
  2. It’s 2 of egg.
  3. It’s 1 envelope of dry onion soup.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of quick oats.
  5. Prepare 2 tbs of milk.
  6. You need 2 packets of beef ramen flavoring.
  7. You need 3 tbs of flour.
  8. Prepare 2 tbs of butter.
  9. Prepare 2 cups of hot water.
  10. You need of Pepper.

Salisbury Steak step by step

  1. Mix ground beef, onion soup, oatmeal, eggs, and milk. Form 4 oval shaped patties..
  2. In a frying pan with oil, brown patties on both sides. Then remove from pan.
  3. Leaving 1 tbs of fat in the pan, add butter. Melt. Add flour. Cook til bubbling..
  4. Meanwhile add beef flavoring to hot water. Add to pan. Put patties back in pan. Boil til sauce thickens and patties are cooked through. Flip patties half way..
