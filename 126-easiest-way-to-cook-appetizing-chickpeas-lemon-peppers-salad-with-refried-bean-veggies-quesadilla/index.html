Easiest Way to Cook Appetizing Chickpeas Lemon Peppers Salad with Refried Bean Veggies Quesadilla

Chickpeas Lemon Peppers Salad with Refried Bean Veggies Quesadilla.

You can have Chickpeas Lemon Peppers Salad with Refried Bean Veggies Quesadilla using 29 ingredients and 8 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Chickpeas Lemon Peppers Salad with Refried Bean Veggies Quesadilla

  1. Prepare of For salad:.
  2. You need of Greens salad as your choice.
  3. You need of Chickpeas rinsed and drained.
  4. It’s of Sliced apple.
  5. You need of Sliced onion.
  6. It’s of Cherry tomatoes (cut in half).
  7. It’s of Seasoning:.
  8. Prepare of Lemon juice.
  9. Prepare of Salt.
  10. It’s of Pepper.
  11. You need of For quesadillas:.
  12. You need of Tortilla (prefer the large one).
  13. You need 1 cup of refried bean.
  14. You need 2 of big mushrooms (sliced).
  15. You need 1/2 of of paprika (sliced).
  16. You need 1/2 cup of chopped onion.
  17. It’s 3 cloves of minced garlic.
  18. It’s 2 of chopped tomatoes.
  19. It’s of Seasoning:.
  20. Prepare 1 tbsp of nutrition yeast.
  21. Prepare 1 tsp of chili powder.
  22. It’s 1/2 tsp of garlic powder.
  23. It’s 1/2 tsp of onion powder (optional).
  24. It’s 1/4 tsp of thyme powder.
  25. It’s 1/4 tsp of turmeric powder.
  26. It’s 1/2 tsp of paprika.
  27. You need 1/2 tsp of smoke paprika.
  28. Prepare 1/4 tsp of celery seed.
  29. You need 1/2 tsp of dry parsley.

Chickpeas Lemon Peppers Salad with Refried Bean Veggies Quesadilla instructions

  1. For salad: Combine all ingredients add lemon juice, pepper, and salt then tossing together. Set aside..
  2. For quesadillas: Heat up the pan, add onion and garlic sauté it until soft. Splash with water to avoid burn..
  3. Add mushrooms, paprika, and tomatoes. Keep sauted and add little bit of water. Simmer until the veggies are tender and the water cooked off..
  4. Add the refried bean, and all the seasoning. Mixed well along with veggies and keep sauted until no more liquid. Tastes it before turn off the heat..
  5. To assembly: Places 1 tortilla on the cutting board, divide the filling among the tortillas spreading evenly then topped with another tortilla. Pressing down lightly to hold it together..
  6. Place the quesadillas in a large non-stick skillet over medium heat. Cook until lightly browned on both sides, turning once for about 3 minutes per side..
  7. To serve, cut each quesadillas into 4 wedge and arrange on the plate along with chickpeas lemon pepper salad. And enjoy..
  8. ❤️Happy Cooking❤️.
