Pumkin alu ke spice chap kabab step by step

  1. Pumkin ko wash karke skin utar ke or roughly chopped karde.
  2. Cooker me pani add karke boil karde boil hone ke baad thanda hone de or hath se nichod de taki sara pani nikal jaye.
  3. Sare chize ek plate me rakhe or achhe se mix kare swad anusar salt add karke dough ready karle or wooden stick pe lagade.
  4. Like this or uper se corn flour sprinkle kare.
  5. Sari stick pe achhe se corn flour lagade properly taki crispy bane kabab.
  6. Nonstick tave pe butter/oil se greece kare or chaap ko aage piche roasted karte rahe taki pura achhe se crispy bane.
  7. Ye pura brown ho jayega sikane ke baad ab gas off karde.
  8. Aapki pumkin chap ready he.
  9. Tomato sauce or onion ke saath serve kar sakte he.
  10. Green chutney or salad ke saath bhi serve kar sakte he.
