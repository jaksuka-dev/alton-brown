Steam Soft Tofu. Steamed tofu with a savory soy sauce garlic dressing. As one of the most popular and humble Chinese ingredients, we eat tofu around the year. It is one of the best ingredients for a simple tofu salad in hot. О steam поддержка. Установить Steam.

Steps to Take on How to Steam Tofu. Steaming is considered to be the healthiest and easiest way to cook tofu. Any other tofu type that is softer than that will become mushy when pressed. You can cook Steam Soft Tofu using 3 ingredients and 2 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Steam Soft Tofu

  1. It’s 2 package of soft tofu.
  2. You need 1 stalk of spring onion.
  3. It’s Half of bowl ground pork.

This easy steamed tofu is my go-to recipe when I am in a hurry to prepare a quick dish for lunch/dinner. It only needs very basic ingredients, like garlic, scallions, and two kinds of soy sauce. Take the tofu package out of the simmering water and open one side of the package top to drink all the liquid inside. Soft, silky tofu is steamed with a delicious ginger soy sauce, topped with colourful julienned vegetables…it’ll convert any “tofu haters” I promise!

Steam Soft Tofu step by step

  1. Marinate ground beef with a little flour and soy sauce mix properly. Then stir fry until done.
  2. Heat wok with water and let it boil. Steam tofu for 5 mins. When done pour the cooked pork on top.
