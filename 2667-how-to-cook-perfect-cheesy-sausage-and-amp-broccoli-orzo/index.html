Ingredients of Cheesy Sausage & Broccoli Orzo

  1. You need 1 1/2 cups of uncooked orzo.
  2. Prepare 2 Tbsp. of olive oil.
  3. It’s 1 of small onion, chopped.
  4. It’s 1 cup of broccoli florets.
  5. Prepare 1 (16 oz.) of pkg. smoked sausage.
  6. You need 1/3 cup of milk.
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese.
  8. Prepare 2 tsp. of garlic powder.
  9. You need to taste of salt and pepper.

Cheesy Sausage & Broccoli Orzo instructions

  1. In a large pot, begin to cook orzo as directed on package..
  2. Coin the sausage link into bite size pieces and saute with onion and garlic powder in the olive oil in a large pot..
  3. Continue cooking until thoroughly cooked and onions are softened..
  4. When pasta is within 5 minutes of being cooked, add in broccoli and continue boil..
  5. Drain pasta and return to pot..
  6. Add in milk, cheddar cheese and salt and pepper. Combine until cheese is melted..
  7. Fold in sausage mixture and combine thoroughly..
  8. Enjoy!.
