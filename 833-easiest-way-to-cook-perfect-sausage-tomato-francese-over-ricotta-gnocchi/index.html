Ingredients of Sausage tomato francese over ricotta gnocchi

  1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of ricotta cheese.
  2. It’s 1 cup of flour.
  3. It’s 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese.
  4. Prepare 2 of Italian sausages.
  5. Prepare 1 cup of chicken stock or broth.
  6. Prepare 1 of lemon.
  7. It’s 1 tablespoon of corn starch.
  8. It’s 1 of tomato.
  9. It’s 3 tablespoons of butter.
  10. Prepare of Chives or parsley for garnish.

Sausage tomato francese over ricotta gnocchi instructions

  1. Combine butter, chicken stock, sausages, juice of 1/4 lemon and 2 slices of lemon to a large pan. Braise same covered on low heat for 45 min..
  2. Drain ricotta on paper towels or cheesecloth..
  3. Mix ricotta, parmesan and flour. Knead until dough like..
  4. Roll into long thin strips. Press with fork. Cut in 1 inch pieces..
  5. Peel, seed and dice tomato..
  6. After sausage has braised for 45 min add gnocchi and tomato to sausage pot..
  7. Garnish with chives or parsley..
