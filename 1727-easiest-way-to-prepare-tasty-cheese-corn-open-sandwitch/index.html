Ingredients of Cheese corn open sandwitch

  1. You need of brown bread.
  2. It’s of grated mozrella cheese.
  3. Prepare of boil corn.
  4. You need of chopped onion.
  5. Prepare of chopped capsicum.
  6. Prepare of Salt and pepper.
  7. You need of Chilli flakes and oregano.
  8. Prepare of tandoori mayonise.

Cheese corn open sandwitch instructions

  1. In a bowl mix mayonise and chopped vegetables.
  2. Add boil corn and mix well.
  3. Add salt and pepper.
  4. Take bread slices.
  5. Spread mixture on it.
  6. Spread grated cheese on it.
  7. Cook on non stick pan on sim.
  8. Cover it.
  9. When cheese melt off the gas.
  10. Sprinkle chilli flakes and oregano.
