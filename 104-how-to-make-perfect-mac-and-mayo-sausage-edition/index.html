Mac and Mayo ( sausage edition ) step by step

  1. boil the cup of macaroni with three cups of water mixed with pepper, salt , and a bit of oil till mac boils well.
  2. at the same time boil the sausages till they become tender..
  3. next cut the onion capsicum chilli and ginger into thin pieces.
  4. the sausages will be done much quicker so when it is cut them into small pieces as well.
  5. in a pan mix the sausages onion capsicum chilli and ginger with small amount of oil till it gets done well! note: reduce the heat do not let the ingredients get done too much.
  6. once the mac is done. drain its liquid and mix the mac with the contents in the pan.
  7. mix well. and add required amounts of mayo!.
  8. enjoy! :)).
