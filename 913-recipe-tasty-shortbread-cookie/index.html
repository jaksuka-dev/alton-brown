Shortbread cookie step by step

  1. Mix butter and sugar until creamy – about 5 min.
  2. Stir in flour but gently so as to not overwork dough.
  3. Press flat with fingertips in a 12×8 pan and prick with a fork several times..
  4. Bake for 35 min at 350 degrees.
