Ingredients of French pancakes

  1. Prepare 1 cups of sifted flour.
  2. Prepare 1/2 teaspoons of salt.
  3. You need Dash of nutmeg or cinnamon.
  4. It’s 3 of egg yolks.
  5. You need 1 cups of milk.
  6. It’s 3 of egg whites.
  7. Prepare 2 tablespoons of butter melted over hot water.

French pancakes instructions

  1. Sift flour and salt and nutmeg.
  2. Beat egg whites till stiff.
  3. Beat egg yolks.
  4. Add milk and butter to egg yolks.
  5. Then add to flour mix well.
  6. Fold in egg whites.
  7. Cook in hot greased pan or griddle.
  8. Top with orange marmalade,current jelly,maple sugar mixed with a little cream or your favorite topping..
