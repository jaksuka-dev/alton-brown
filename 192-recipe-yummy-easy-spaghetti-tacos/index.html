Easy spaghetti tacos!!!!!! instructions

  1. Prepare noodles according to package directions. Drain and return to pot..
  2. Brown meat in pan until no longer pink. Add taco seasoning and water. Bring to a boil.( you can add vegetable to the meat it is optional).
  3. To assemble the tacos, place a spoonful of salsa in the shell. Sprinkle with a small amount of cheese and tadaaaaa!! Very messy, but very yummy.
