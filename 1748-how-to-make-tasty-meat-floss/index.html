How to Make Tasty Meat floss

  • Alton Brown
  • Monday, Jul 6, 2020

Meat floss. Meat floss has an interesting name in Chinese language: 肉松 literally means fluffy meat. The most impressive image in my mind is the soft floss buns. Just with one bite, you can taste the savory pork.

However, the meat floss sold on the market is mostly oil and grease, and does not have the best original flavor of the meat. Floss is a very good kid’s food supplement. Meat floss was definitely not on the menu. You can cook Meat floss using 9 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Meat floss

  1. You need of Fried Sallah beef meat.
  2. You need of Scotch bonnet.
  3. It’s of Onions.
  4. You need of Seasoning.
  5. You need of White pepper.
  6. It’s Pinch of Salt.
  7. Prepare of Water.
  8. Prepare of Oil.
  9. It’s of Garlic.

I may be the rare vegetarian able to tell you this from personal experience: Meat floss is sweet like cotton candy, but salty at the same time. It may sound gross but hear me out. Also referred to as Dried Shredded Pork (beef may be used also but is tougher). Learn how to easily make this Asian bakery style pork floss buns using tang zhong and autolyse method to make the softest fluffiest Super Soft Meat/Pork Floss Milk Buns (Tang Zhong Method).

Meat floss step by step

  1. Add water in a pot then add the beef meat followed by Chopped 1 Medium size onion and put them on fire until all the water has dried..
  2. Allow the meat to cool a little bit then make sure there is no longer any water on the meat.
  3. Wash and rinse the Onions and Scotch bonnet and put them in a mortar and crush them very well with a pistle.
  4. Add the garlic in the mortar and crush followed by the seasonings white pepper and a pinch of salt also until they’re soft.
  5. Then add the meat and crush all together. Make sure you Crush the meat with the pistle very well.
  6. Add some little oil in a frying pan and put it on fire and then add the meat and continue stirring the meat until your desired consistency is achieved.
  7. Note :Dont leave the meat on fire even for a minute.Continue stirring the meat until you’re done.

Meat Floss (Beef Floss) – Here is another Northern Nigerian delicacy which is enjoyed by the majority. This is a vei delicious dried shredded Meat floss. Rousong, also called meat floss, pork floss, or pork sung, is a dried Chinese meat item that has a light and fluffy texture similar to coarse cotton. Rousong is used as a topping for many foods such as. Pork, dried meat floss, fish products, canned foods, canned meat, fruit preserves, ketchup, jams, pickles, sauerkraut, soup, eggs, milk beverages (milk based), milk products, edible oils, raisins, potato.
