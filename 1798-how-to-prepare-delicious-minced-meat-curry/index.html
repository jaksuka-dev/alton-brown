Minced meat curry instructions

  1. Peel potatoes and cut into wedges and boil slightly using salt as you prepare the mince curry. Add juice of 1½ lime into the raw meat before cooking and set aside..
  2. In a cooking pot fry onions in oil. Add green pepper..
  3. Add crushed garlic ginger paste after onions become translucent..
  4. Go in with turmeric powder, curry powder an black pepper. Mix well..
  5. Pour in blended tomatoes followed by your tomato paste. Let it simmer for a minute.
  6. Add water and let it simmer for 3 minutes then add in your mince beef and let it cook till done..
  7. After the mince is done cooking pour in lemon juice followed by the boiled potatoes. Chop some fresh coriander then switch of heat. Mix them in and let it steam for the flavours to infuse into the potatoes..
  8. Serve with rice, pasta or chapati and enjoy..
