How to Make Yummy Chili Sesame Avocado & Brown Sushi Rice Bowl for 1

  • Alton Brown
  • Wednesday, Feb 5, 2020

Chili Sesame Avocado & Brown Sushi Rice Bowl for 1.

You can have Chili Sesame Avocado & Brown Sushi Rice Bowl for 1 using 11 ingredients and 2 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Chili Sesame Avocado & Brown Sushi Rice Bowl for 1

  1. It’s 1 cup of sushi rice.
  2. You need of For the Topping:.
  3. It’s 1 cup of diced (roughly 1/2-inch cubes), ripe avocado.
  4. You need 2-3 Tablespoons of minced onion.
  5. Prepare 2 Tablespoons of diced tomato.
  6. It’s 1-2 Tablespoons of minced fresh chilies (such as jalapeño or serrano).
  7. It’s 1/2 Tablespoon of sriracha or chili garlic sauce.
  8. You need 1 Tablespoon of soy sauce.
  9. Prepare 1-2 teaspoons of toasted sesame oil.
  10. Prepare 1 squeeze of lemon juice (about 2 teaspoons).
  11. It’s of optional: 1/4 cup roasted cashews (salted or unsalted – whichever you prefer) for extra good fat and protein in your meal.

Chili Sesame Avocado & Brown Sushi Rice Bowl for 1 step by step

  1. Combine topping ingredients in a medium bowl and toss gently until they’re thoroughly and evenly incorporated..
  2. Serve atop your sushi rice and enjoy. :).
