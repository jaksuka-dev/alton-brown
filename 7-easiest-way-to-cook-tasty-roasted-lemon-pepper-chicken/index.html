Easiest Way to Cook Tasty Roasted Lemon Pepper Chicken

  • Alton Brown
  • Wednesday, Feb 5, 2020

Roasted Lemon Pepper Chicken.

You can cook Roasted Lemon Pepper Chicken using 9 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Roasted Lemon Pepper Chicken

  1. Prepare 2 of whole chickens.
  2. Prepare to taste of Lemon pepper seasoning.
  3. You need to taste of Black pepper.
  4. It’s to taste of Sazon (sin achiote).
  5. You need to taste of Oregano.
  6. You need to taste of White vinegar.
  7. Prepare as needed of Vegetable oil.
  8. It’s 10-20 cloves of garlic, minced.
  9. It’s to taste of Parsley flakes.

Roasted Lemon Pepper Chicken step by step

  1. Rinse and pat dry your whole chickens (recipe can also be used to cook only 1 chicken, my family eats ALOT of my cooking so I’m making 2 chickens today !) lol 😂………
  2. Place your chickens in a large baking pan……..
  3. For this recipe I normally don’t measure out the ingredients, I just sprinkle everything evenly over both sides of the chicken, then rub it all in, including the cavity, then I place the minced garlic on top of the chicken, topped off with some sprinkled parsley flakes………At this point you may refrigerate the chicken overnight so it has time to marinate fully……..
  4. Roast chicken(s), uncovered, in a preheated 400 degree oven for 1 hour 15 minutes…….Baste every 30 minutes……..
  5. Let cool, then serve with your favorite side dish and enjoy 😉!!! You can also serve with my Firecracker Red Potatoes as your side dish !!! 😁😁😁😁😁😁.
