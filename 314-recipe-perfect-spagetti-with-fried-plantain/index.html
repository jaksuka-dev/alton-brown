Spagetti with fried plantain instructions

  1. Slice plantain and fry in a hot vegetable oil..
  2. Boil the spagetti for 7mins.wash and keep.
  3. Heat up the pot,pour in the vegetable oil after 40seconds pour in slice onion..
  4. Open the tomato paste and add.fry for 2mins..
  5. Pour in water for the cooking and boil for 3mins..
  6. Uncover add fresh pepper,salt,maggi cubes,curry,garlic and turmeric.cook back to boil for another 3mins..
  7. Then pour in the parboiled spagetti stir very well and cook.boil for 10mins..
  8. After 10mins the spagetti should be soft by now.but make the water has dryed..
  9. Stir for 30seconds and allow it to dry..
