Caramel Cookies Cake instructions

  1. Beat butter, brown sigar and salt with mixer. Add in sugar & egg. Add in ③ and ④. Make sure it doesnt beat for too long or else it will be watery.
  2. Prepare Caramel sauce. Double boil your heavy cream and salt. Heat up your sugar and water until it become golden brown color. Add in your heavy cream before it cool down. Add on butter and mix well..
  3. Bake it for 25 – 30 minutes, 165/150℃.
