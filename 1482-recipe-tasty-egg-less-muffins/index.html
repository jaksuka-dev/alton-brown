Egg less Muffins instructions

  1. Take sugar powder in a bowl. Add melted butter, baking powder, sweet soda and vanilla flavour.Beat mixture through hand blender..
  2. Now add maida and beat. Finally add milk dahi, crushed kaju & Tutti frutti. Beat nicely. Muffin mixture is ready..
  3. Grease muffin mould and fill mixture as shown in pic..
  4. Put the filled muffin moulds inside preheated OTG @ 180 degree and bake for approx 35 minutes..
  5. Egg less Muffins are ready..
