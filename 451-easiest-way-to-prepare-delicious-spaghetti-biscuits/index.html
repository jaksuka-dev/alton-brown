Easiest Way to Prepare Delicious Spaghetti Biscuits

  • Alton Brown
  • Friday, Jan 31, 2020

Spaghetti Biscuits.

You can have Spaghetti Biscuits using 19 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Spaghetti Biscuits

  1. You need of Biscuits.
  2. Prepare 4 cup of self rising flour.
  3. You need 1/4 cup of peanut oil.
  4. It’s 1 teaspoon of salt.
  5. It’s 1-1/4 cup of milk.
  6. Prepare 2 tablespoon of water.
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup of apple cider vinegar.
  8. You need 2 tablespoons of butter.
  9. Prepare 1 teaspoon of granulated garlic powder.
  10. You need As needed of Lyles Golden Syrup optional.
  11. It’s To taste of butter for the split Biscuits.
  12. Prepare of Spaghetti.
  13. Prepare 2/3 pound of spaghetti noodles that’s what I had leftover.
  14. Prepare 48 ounces of mixed spaghetti sauce.
  15. It’s 2/3 pound of porkloin.
  16. You need To taste of salt.
  17. You need To taste of granulated garlic powder.
  18. You need As needed of grated Parmesan cheese.
  19. Prepare To taste of red pepper flakes.

Spaghetti Biscuits instructions

  1. Preheat oven 450°Fahrenheit. To a mixing bowl add oil and salt. Add the apple cider vinegar to a measuring cup mix the milk and vinegar..
  2. Add flour to the oil and salt. Pour the milk mixture to the flour and mix well. Form into 9 Biscuits and bake for 10 minutes. Melt the butter and mix with garlic..
  3. Take Biscuits out. Brush the tops of Biscuits with the butter garlic mixture. Put under the broiler till browned..
  4. Split in half add some butter to taste. Cut the loin up into chunks remove any membrane or sinews..
  5. Dice the pork season and put into a deep pan and sauté for 12 minutes. Get the sauces. Open and pour in add a tablespoon of water to each jar. Put lid on top and shake it to get leftover sauce out and pour it into the sauce..
  6. Simmer the sauce for 7 minutes. Add pasta to your plate, then cover with cheese. Add the sauce on top. Add more cheese on the sauce and pepper flakes on top. Serve I hope you enjoy!!.
