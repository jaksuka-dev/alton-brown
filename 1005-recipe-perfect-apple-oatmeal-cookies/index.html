Ingredients of Apple Oatmeal Cookies

  1. It’s 1 cup of flour.
  2. You need 1 tsp. of nutmeg.
  3. It’s 1 tsp. of cinnamon.
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp. of baking soda.
  5. You need 1/4 tsp. of salt.
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup of butter (1 stick), softened.
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup of brown sugar.
  8. You need 1/4 cup of sugar.
  9. It’s 1 of egg.
  10. Prepare 1 tsp. of vanilla extract.
  11. Prepare 1 3/4 cup of oats.
  12. Prepare 1 of medium apple, peeled, cored & small chopped.

Apple Oatmeal Cookies instructions

  1. In a bowl mix together flour, nutmeg, cinnamon, baking soda and salt. Set aside..
  2. Chop apple and set aside..
  3. In mixer, cream together butter and sugars..
  4. Add egg and vanilla..
  5. Slowly add dry ingredients to mix until well blended..
  6. Hand mix in the oats and apples..
  7. Cover and place in fridge for 30 minutes. Hand roll abd place on parchment lined baking sheets..
  8. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes, or until toothpick inserted into middle of cookie comes out clean..
  9. Enjoy!.
