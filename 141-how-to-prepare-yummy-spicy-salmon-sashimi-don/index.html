Ingredients of Spicy Salmon Sashimi Don

  1. It’s 200 g of Sashimi-grade Fresh Salmon *bones removed, sliced.
  2. It’s 1/4 of Red Onion *finely sliced.
  3. Prepare 1-2 handfuls of Vegetables *Today I used ‘Shungiku’.

  5. It’s 2 of servings Freshly Cooked Rice.
  6. Prepare 1/2 sheet of Nori (Seaweed Sheet) *cut into thin strips OR torn into small pieces.

  8. It’s 1 clove of Garlic *grated.
