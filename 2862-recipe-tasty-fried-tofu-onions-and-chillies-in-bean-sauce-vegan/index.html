Fried tofu, onions and chillies in bean sauce #Vegan# instructions

  1. Slice tofu, carrot, pepper and onion, and set aside. Mince garlic..
  2. Stir fry carrot and onion in olive oil until aromatic for about 3 minutes..
  3. Add fried tofu, garlic and sauté for another minute..
  4. Add soy sauce and bean paste and a Tsp of water and keep stir fry for about a minute until the sauce well coat all ingredients..
  5. Add chilli pepper in the end to avoid overcooking and cook for 30 more second. Stir in starch water right after. Adjust seasoning if necessary..
