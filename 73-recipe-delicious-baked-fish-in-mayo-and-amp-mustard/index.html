Recipe: Delicious Baked Fish in Mayo & Mustard

  • Alton Brown
  • Saturday, Jan 25, 2020

Baked Fish in Mayo & Mustard.

You can have Baked Fish in Mayo & Mustard using 6 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Baked Fish in Mayo & Mustard

  1. You need 1 lb of White Fish.
  2. It’s 3 tbsp of Mayo.
  3. Prepare 1 tsp of Dry Mustard.
  4. You need 1/2 cup of Chopped Onion.
  5. Prepare 1 dash of Cayenne.
  6. Prepare 1/8 tsp of Dillweed.

Baked Fish in Mayo & Mustard instructions

  1. Preheat Oven to 400°F.
  2. Place Fish in Lightly Greased Shallow Baking Dish.
  3. Combine Mayo, Mustard, Onion, Cayenne, and Dillweed in a Bowl until Blended.
  4. Spread Mixture Evenly onto the Fish.
  5. Bake at 400°F for 18-22 Minutes until Fish Flakes Easily.
