Ingredients of Biscuits with tahini and almond

  1. You need 2 cup of flour.
  2. It’s 100 grams of almond.
  3. It’s 150 grams of butter.
  4. It’s 150 grams of sugar powder.
  5. Prepare 150 grams of tahini paste.
  6. Prepare 5 tbsp of water.
  7. Prepare 1 of vanilla.
  8. You need pinch of salt.

No need to miss out, whip up a super quick batch of these delicious grain-free cookies. A wide variety of almond biscuits options are available to you, such as taste, flavor, and texture. Add almond meal and stir until well-combined. In a large bowl, combine tahini and honey and stir until combined.

Biscuits with tahini and almond instructions

  1. In a bowl with hands mix flour + 80% of ground almonds + butter cut into cubes + sugar + vanilla + salt.
  2. When you have a crumbly mixture, add water and tahini and continue kneading until having smooth mass (Add more water if you are not having smooth mass yet)..
  3. Make small forms of the dough, add an almond over each form and put to oven until golden brown..
