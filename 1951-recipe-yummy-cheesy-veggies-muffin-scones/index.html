Ingredients of Cheesy veggies muffin scones

  1. You need 1 cup of all purpose flour.
  2. It’s 2 tsp of baking powder.
  3. Prepare 1 tsp of salt.
  4. You need 1 cup of curd.
  5. You need 1/4 cup of chopped onion.
  6. It’s 1/4 cup of chopped capsicum.
  7. You need 1/4 cup of cottage cheese.
  8. It’s 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese.
  9. It’s 1/4 cup of butter.
  10. It’s 1/2 tsp of chilli flakes.
  11. It’s 1/2 tsp of oregano.

Cheesy veggies muffin scones instructions

  1. Sieve and mix all the dry ingredients together..
  2. Whisk the curd with butter and mix with the flour along with the veggies, cottage cheese, chilli flakes and oregano..
  3. Mix all the ingredients..
  4. Grease a muffin mould with butter and pour the mixture till half of the mould..
  5. Then add some grated cheese over it..
  6. Then bake it in the preheated microwave for 15 minutes in 150degree Celsius..
  7. Now your muffin scones are ready..
  8. Put some oregano over it and serve hot..
