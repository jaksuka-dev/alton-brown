italian spaghetti and meatballs from scratch instructions

  1. mince or chop onion in food processor dont over chop.
  2. slice mushrooms to desired thickness.
  3. put mushrooms aside for later.
  4. preheat oven to 350.
  5. put turkey in med. to lg. sized mixing bowl.
  6. mix all seasoning together taste to see if it needs more salt always taste before adding to meat.
  7. add seasoning to meat and mix well.
  8. cook for 20 mins. turning once half way through, spread oil on cookie sheet and coat meatballs evenly with oil so they dont stick.
  9. heat oil in sauce pan for mushrooms add mushrooms and seasoning and cook on med. to low heat until aldente.
  10. 10mins into cooking meatballs start boiling water add pinch of salt to water add noodles when water is boiling.
  11. drain water from noodles.
  12. heat sauce.
  13. assemble all ingreadients together for one delicious plate of spaghetti.
  14. if you desire make a dipping sauce for bread out of italian seasoning and olive oil 1/4 cup oil and 3 tablespoons seasoning mix together and serve with a hot bagette.
