Tofu Sisig. Crispy Tofu Sisig is a dish composed of deep fried tofu cubes, chopped chili peppers, and onion. It is healthier alternative to traditional pork sisig because it does not use any meat and greasy ingredients. Aside from being healthy, this crispy tofu sisig recipe is also vegan.

Ingredients of Tofu Sisig

  1. It’s 500 g of firm tofu cut into cubes.
  2. Prepare 1 cup of canola or cooking oil.
  3. It’s 3-4 cloves of minced garlic.
  4. It’s 1/2 cup of diced onions.
  5. It’s 2 of chopped green long chilis with the seeds removed.
  6. Prepare 1/2 of diced green bell peppers.
  7. It’s 1/2 cup of mayonnaise.
  8. Prepare 3 tablespoons of oyster sauce.
  9. It’s 2 tablespoons of water.
  10. It’s to taste of Salt and pepper.
  11. It’s of Optional: calamansi and fried egg.

Tofu Sisig step by step

  1. Deep fry tofu until golden brown and crunchy. Drain oil and set aside..
  2. Sautè garlic and onions until onions are translucent..
  3. Add mayonnaise, oyster sauce and water. Stir and simmer on low fire for about 2 minutes until it slightly thickens..
  4. Add fried tofu and bell peppers. Season with salt and pepper. Cook for another 2-3 minutes..
  5. Serve on a sizzling plate with calamansi. Optional: serve with fried egg on top with rice on the side..
