How to Prepare Yummy Home Made Shawarma with Lacchay dar Paratha

  • Alton Brown
  • Tuesday, Jan 7, 2020

Home Made Shawarma with Lacchay dar Paratha.

You can cook Home Made Shawarma with Lacchay dar Paratha using 20 ingredients and 10 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Home Made Shawarma with Lacchay dar Paratha

  1. Prepare of For Dough :.
  2. Prepare 4 Cup of flour.
  3. It’s 2 tbsp of ghee.
  4. You need 1/2 tsp of Salt.
  5. It’s of Warm water for Kneeding.
  6. Prepare of For Chiken marination:.
  7. Prepare 1 Cup of Chicken in cube.
  8. It’s 1/2 cup of yogurt.
  9. You need 1 tbsp of sirka.
  10. You need 1 tbsp of Soy Sauce.
  11. It’s 1 1/2 tbsp of lehsan adrak paste.
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp of Salt.
  13. You need 2 tbsp of Oil.
  14. You need 2 tsp of Garm masala.
  15. You need of for shawarma filling:.
  16. It’s of Chopped fried cabbage.
  17. Prepare of Chopped fried Capsicum.
  18. You need of chooped Fried Onion.
  19. Prepare of Mayonnaise as need.
  20. Prepare of ketchup as requirement.

Home Made Shawarma with Lacchay dar Paratha instructions

  1. Aata achi tarha goondh k dough bana len.
  2. Pehle dough spread karen phir roll karte jayen.
  3. Phir usko pairhay ki shakal den.
  4. Again spread kar k paratha bailen. aur dono side se saiken..
  5. Ghee apply karen aur golden brown kar len.Now its ready..
  6. Chiken cube kar len phir tamam merination masalon ka batter bana k chiken me add kar k 1,2 hr k liye fridge me rakh den..
  7. Phir isay pateelay me charha den aur cook kar len.
  8. Tamam vegetable cut kar k light oil me fry karen halka sa salt n black pepper add karden..
  9. Parathay me mayonnaise spread karen ketchup apply karen then vegetable chicken rakhen aur fold kar len..
  10. Now its ready to eat..
