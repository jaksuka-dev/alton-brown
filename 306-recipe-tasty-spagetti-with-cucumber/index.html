Spagetti with cucumber instructions

  1. Boil the spagetti for 10minutes and wash keep aside..
  2. Cut the fresh pepper,fresh tomatoes and onion.heat up the vegetable oil and pour the onion,fresh pepper and fresh tomatoes..
  3. Fry for 5minutes,add the curry,Seasoning,salt,ginger and garlic powder fry for 3 more minutes..
