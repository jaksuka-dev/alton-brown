Ingredients of 3 cups mushrooms

  1. It’s 150 grams of mushroom.
  2. Prepare 2 tablespoons of sesame oil.
  3. You need 2 tablespoons of soy sauce.
  4. You need 2 tablespoons of cooking sake.
  5. It’s 0.5 tablespoon of sugar.
  6. Prepare 3 cloves of garlic.
  7. It’s 10 g of ginger.
  8. Prepare As needed of Chili (optional).

3 cups mushrooms step by step

  1. Prepare mushroom, garlic and ginger.
  2. Put 1 tablespoon of sesame oil and ginger in the pot before turning on the stove, add garlic in after you smell the ginger.
  3. Add mushrooms and stir fry until some water come out of the mushroom. Add chili and stir fry for 5-10 secs(optional).
  4. Mix 1 tablespoon of sesame oil, 2 tablespoons of soy sauce and cooking sake, half tablespoon of sugar before pouring it along the edge of the pot.
  5. Stir fry for 5-10 secs. Turn off the heat then add the basel.
  6. Stirring a bit before serving. Voilà ❤️.
