Ingredients of Chicken Sausage skillet dinner

  1. Prepare 2 Tbsp of Olive oil.
  2. It’s 1/4 C of chopped onion.
  3. It’s 1/4 C of chopped sweet peppers.
  4. Prepare 2 (6 oz) of chopped chicken sausage.
  5. It’s 2 of Kale leaves, finely chopped (optional).
  6. Prepare 2 tsp of Italian seasoning.
  7. You need 2 C of chicken stock/broth.
  8. Prepare 1 1/4 C of water (divided 1C & 1/4C).
  9. You need 4 oz of (ish) dry veggy rotini pasta.
  10. It’s 1 Tbsp of cornstarch.

Chicken Sausage skillet dinner instructions

  1. Heat Olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and sautee for 2min, then add sweet peppers and cook, stirring, until onions caramelize a bit..
  3. Add chicken sausage and cook for 3 min. Sprinkle italian seasoning in and stir to combine..
  4. Pour in Chicken stock and and 1 cup of the water, stir in the kale, then add the pasta. Bring to a boil, turn down heat to low boil and cover. Cook for 10min, or until the noodles are done to your liking..
  5. *You may need to add water, keep the noodles in water to cook..
  6. Add cornstarch to 1/4 cold water – then pour into skillet, keeping a low boil until sauce sauce thickens a bit. Sauce will thicken a bit more when removed, FYI..
  7. Enjoy!!!.
