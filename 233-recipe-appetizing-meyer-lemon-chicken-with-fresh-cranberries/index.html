Recipe: Appetizing Meyer Lemon Chicken with Fresh Cranberries

  • Alton Brown
  • Monday, May 25, 2020

Meyer Lemon Chicken with Fresh Cranberries.

You can cook Meyer Lemon Chicken with Fresh Cranberries using 5 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Meyer Lemon Chicken with Fresh Cranberries

  1. Prepare 4 of large chicken thighs, skin on, bone-in.
  2. It’s 2 of table spoons olive oil.
  3. It’s of salt and freshly ground pepper.
  4. It’s 1 of Meyer lemon, thinly sliced.
  5. It’s 1 cup of fresh cranberries.

Meyer Lemon Chicken with Fresh Cranberries instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 325 degrees F..
  2. Heat the oil in a large ovenproof skillet over medium-high heat. Sprinkle the chicken thighs liberally with salt and pepper on both sides..
  3. Lay the chicken skin side down into the hot skillet. Cook without moving for 5-7 minutes, until the skin is golden brown and crisp..
  4. Turn the chicken thighs over and turn off the heat. Lay one beautiful slice of Meyer lemon on top of each piece of chicken. Scatter the remaining lemon slices and the fresh cranberries around the skillet..
  5. Slide the skillet into the oven. Roast the chicken for 50 minutes, until it is cooked through and the lemon slices on top have begun to caramelize. Remove the skillet from the oven. Serve immediately..
