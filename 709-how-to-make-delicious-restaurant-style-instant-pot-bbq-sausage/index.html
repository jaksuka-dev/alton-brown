Ingredients of Restaurant Style Instant Pot BBQ Sausage

  1. It’s 1/2 package of “Zumos Party Time Smoked Sausage Links” (6 Links); Sliced.
  2. It’s 1 cup of “Sweet Baby Ray’s Original” BBQ Sauce.
  3. Prepare 3 tbl of Grape Jelly.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of water.

Restaurant Style Instant Pot BBQ Sausage instructions

  1. In your Instant Pot, combine BBQ sauce, water, and grape jelly – whisk well (add more water if it looks too thick to avoid a burn warning!).
  2. Slice 6 Zumos Smoked Sausage Links (at an angle to be fancy).
  3. Add sliced sausage to instant pot.
  4. Mix well and again, add more water if it’s too thick!.
  5. Lock the lid on your Instant Pot and set to Manual Pressure Cook, High, for 10 minutes.
  6. Natural or quick release and voila!.
