Keto Peanut Butter Cookies instructions

  1. Preheat oven 350. I a mixing bowl combine all ingredients and mix until well combined..
  2. Scoop out dough and form balls. Place on baking sheet and press with a fork forming a criss cross pattern. Bake 13-15 minutes, until the tops are set and golden. Let cookies cool for 10 minutes. Enjoy!.
