Ingredients of Lemon Herb Quinoa

  1. Prepare 1 cup of white quinoa.
  2. It’s 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  3. It’s 2 cups of water.
  4. You need 1 tsp of oregano.
  5. It’s 1/2 tsp of thyme.
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp of rosemary.
  7. You need 2 tbsp of lemon juice.
  8. Prepare 3 tbsp of fresh chopped parsley.
  9. It’s 1 cup of baby spinach.
  10. Prepare of Fresh ground pepper.
  11. It’s to taste of Salt.

Lemon Herb Quinoa step by step

  1. In a medium nonstick saucepan, heat olive oil on medium high heat and toast quinoa until “popping,” about 4-5 minutes.
  2. Add water, oregano, thyme, and rosemary, then simmer covered for 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in lemon juice, parsley, salt, pepper, and spinach, then cover and simmer until spinach is wilted.
