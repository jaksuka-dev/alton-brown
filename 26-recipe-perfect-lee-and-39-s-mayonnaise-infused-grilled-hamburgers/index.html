Ingredients of Lee’s Mayonnaise Infused Grilled Hamburgers

  1. It’s 1 Pound of Ground Beef (80/20).
  2. It’s 2 Tablespoons of your favorite Mayonnaise.
  3. Prepare 1 Tablespoon of Worcestershire Sauce.
  4. Prepare 1 of \2 Teaspoon Seasoning Salt.
  5. Prepare 1 of \2 Teaspoon Fresh Cracked Black Pepper.

Serve on buns with your favorite toppings. Hamburgers with grilled chicken and cole slaw on a wooden board on the table with cards and bingo chips, top view. The Grilled Hamburgers recipe out of our category Meat! Slice buns and toast on the edge of the grill.

Lee’s Mayonnaise Infused Grilled Hamburgers instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together, until just combined. Form into three 5 ounce patty’s. Make a little them indentation in the middle of the patty’s with your thumb. This helps to keep them from shrinking up while grilling. I put them in the refrigerator for an hour or so, to let them firm up..
  2. Prepare grill. Grill over medium high heat, for around 4 minutes per side flipping only once. Add cheese to melt over patty if desired..
  3. Serve with nice butter toasted buns. Garnish with any vegetables and condiments of your choice. Message me with any questions. Enjoy.
