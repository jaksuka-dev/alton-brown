Ingredients of Tofu with Spring Onion

  2. It’s of Few stalks of spring onion.
  3. You need 2 pcs of shallots diced.
  4. It’s 3 cloves of garlic diced.
  5. Prepare 2 slice of ginger minced.
  6. You need 2 tbsp of soy sauce.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of Shaoxing wine (optional).

Tofu with Spring Onion instructions

  2. Stir fry the spring onions, ginger, garlic and shallots until aroma and pour in Shaoxing wine. Caution, flame may surface on the wok!.
  3. Add in the fried tofu and add in soy sauce and let the tofu absorb the soy sauce both sides for about 2 mins and serve..
