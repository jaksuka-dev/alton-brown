Steak NY Strip step by step

  1. Preheat oven 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Rinse and dry the steak.
  3. Tenderize the steak I used a hammer. Season one side..
  4. Heat an oven safe pan and add oil when hot add the steaks seasoned side down. Season the unseasoned side that’s up. Sear for 3 minutes. Add the liquid smoke..
  5. Melt the butter and mix well with the garlic. Pour on top of the steak..
  6. Put into oven cook till your desired doneness..
  7. Put under broiler for 3-1/2 minutes on hi..
  8. Take out and let rest in its juices for 7-8 minutes..
  9. Slice and serve I hope you enjoy!.
