You can have Cheese Straws Pastry using 5 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Cheese Straws Pastry

  1. It’s 3 sheets of Puff Pastry.
  2. You need 100 gr of cheddar cheese (finely grated).
  3. You need 100 gr of parmesan cheese (finely grated).
  4. Prepare 50 gr of rosemary leaves (finely chopped).
  5. You need 1 tbsp of margarine.

Using store-bought puff pastry makes them extremely easy. Cheese straws are a time honored party favorite. There are many different recipes that you can use to make them. You can make them from scratch, use pastry dough, or use stale bread.

Cheese Straws Pastry step by step

  1. Preheat the oven 160°C, prepare the baking tray, brush margarine all over the tray, put aside..
  2. Mix the grated cheddar, parmesan cheese and chopped rosemary..
  3. Cut up puff pastry sheets long shaped..
  4. Coat the puff pastry with the cheese mixture one by one and twist them lightly..
  5. Arrange them on the baking tray and bake them for 5-10 mins. (be careful it burns quickly).
  6. When they turn golden, take them out and enjoy them with the loved ones..
