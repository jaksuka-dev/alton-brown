Ingredients of Honey lemon pepper chicken

  1. It’s 2 of chicken breast sliced in half.
  2. Prepare 4 cups of chicken stock.
  3. You need 4 tbsp of honey.
  4. You need 1 tbsp of crushed red pepper.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of soy sauce.
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup of lemon juice.
  7. It’s 2 of large garlic cloves minced.
  8. Prepare of Flat leaf parsley minced.

Honey lemon pepper chicken step by step

  1. Season chicken with a dusting of salt, pepper, onion and garlic powder to taste on both sides and rub with flour.
  2. Sear golden brown on both sides in butter and set aside.
  3. Add chicken stock and simmer scraping the bits.
  4. Add honey, lemon, garlic, soy and reduce.
  5. Add chicken back to the pan and sprinkle parsley and baste the chicken.
  6. Turn up the heat and reduce until thick and then add couple pats of butter and mix and let cool.
  7. Serve with rice and lemon zested asparagus.
