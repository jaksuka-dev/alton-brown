I tasted this masala peanut chaat first at Kulu at the place we stayed. Vj introduced this dish to me and i loved it the very first time I ate. Then during that stay we ordered that several times. You can have Peanut Chaat !! using 9 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Peanut Chaat !!

  1. Prepare of Raw Peanuts.
  2. It’s of big Onion finely chopped.
  3. You need of big Tomato finely chopped.
  4. It’s of Green Chili finely chopped.
  5. It’s of Salt according.
  6. It’s of Chaat Masala.
  7. It’s of Roasted Jeera Powder.
  8. You need of Lemon Juice.
  9. You need of Finely Chopped Green Coriander Leaves.

After that I had no chance of tasting it in any other restaurants. Peanut Chaat is a very delicious and healthy snack prepared with boiled peanuts and fresh vegetables. It is rich in protein and is a perfect tea time treat. Moongphali Chat or Peanut Chaat – Healthy, delicious and no oil Indian snack that can be served with a cup of coffee or chai.

Peanut Chaat !! instructions

  1. Pressure cook the raw peanuts for 1 or 2 whistles. Drain the water and keep aside boiled raw peanuts..
  2. Finely chopped onion, tomato, green chilli and fresh green coriander leaves..
  3. In a mixing bowl take boiled raw peanuts. Add finely chopped onions, tomatoes, green chilli and fresh green coriander leaves..
  4. Add salt, chaat masala, roasted jeera powder and lemon juice. Mix all well..
  5. Garnish with fresh green coriander leaves. Healthy delicious peanut chaat is ready to serve..

Drain the excess water and transfer the peanuts to a wide mixing bowl. Add all the ingredients to it and combine well. Add more chaat masala or lemon juice or red chilli powder if.
