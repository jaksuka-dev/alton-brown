Recipe: Appetizing Lemon Chicken Tacos

  • Alton Brown
  • Saturday, Feb 8, 2020

Lemon Chicken Tacos.

You can have Lemon Chicken Tacos using 9 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Lemon Chicken Tacos

  1. You need 1 lb of shredded chicken.
  2. Prepare 2 C of chicken stock.
  3. You need 1 of lemon; zested & juiced.
  4. You need 1/4 bundle of parsley; chiffonade.
  5. It’s 1 of avocado.
  6. It’s 1 of small shallot; minced.
  7. You need 6 of tortillas.
  8. Prepare as needed of shredded chihuahua cheese.
  9. It’s as needed of kosher salt and black pepper.

Lemon Chicken Tacos step by step

  1. Cover shredded chicken with chicken stock. Add a small pinch of salt and pepper. Slowly heat to a simmer and cover. Do not boil. Simmer for 2-3 minutes, or until chicken is thoroughly heated..
  2. Whisk together avocado, shallot, and half of the lemon juice in a small mixing bowl. Season lightly with salt and black pepper..
  3. Drain chicken and reserve stock. Cover with remaining lemon juice..
  4. Spread a thin layer of the avocado puree on each tortilla to hold the filling in place..
  5. Garnish each taco with lemon zest, shredded cheese, and parsley..
