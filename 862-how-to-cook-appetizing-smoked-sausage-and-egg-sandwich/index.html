How to Cook Appetizing Smoked Sausage and Egg Sandwich

  • Alton Brown
  • Tuesday, Jun 2, 2020

Smoked Sausage and Egg Sandwich.

You can cook Smoked Sausage and Egg Sandwich using 6 ingredients and 8 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Smoked Sausage and Egg Sandwich

  1. You need 3/4 pound of smoked sausage.
  2. You need 3 slices of bread.
  3. You need 1/4 cup of Heinz 57 steak sauce.
  4. It’s 3 of large eggs.
  5. It’s 2 teaspoon of vegetable oil to fry in.
  6. It’s To taste of salt and ground black pepper.

Smoked Sausage and Egg Sandwich instructions

  1. Fry the sausage in oil for 7 minutes turning often..
  2. Split them lengthwise and fry the cut side down..
  3. Move to the side..
  4. Fry your eggs. Add salt and pepper..
  5. Add half the eggs to two different slices of bread.
  6. Add two split sausages to each part of the eggs and Heinz 57 steak sauce on top of the sausages..
  7. Stack the sandwich..
  8. Serve I hope you enjoy!!.
