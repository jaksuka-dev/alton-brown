Recipe: Delicious Indi Sushi

  • Alton Brown
  • Sunday, Mar 1, 2020

Indi Sushi.

You can cook Indi Sushi using 29 ingredients and 12 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Indi Sushi

  1. You need of For rice:.
  2. You need 1/2 cup of basmati rice.
  3. You need 1 of clove.
  4. Prepare 1 of bayleaf.
  5. It’s 2 of cardamom.
  6. Prepare 1 of small piece of cinnamon.
  7. You need 1/4 tsp of fennel seeds crushed.
  8. It’s To taste of Salt.
  9. Prepare of For masala:.
  10. It’s 1 of onion.
  11. Prepare 1 of tomato.
  12. You need 1/2 cup of paneer.
  13. You need 1/4 cup of grated coconut.
  14. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp of ginger garlic paste.
  15. Prepare 1/2 tsp of turmeric powder.
  16. It’s 1 tbsp of coriander powder.
  17. It’s 1/4 tbsp of Kashmiri red chilli powder.
  18. It’s 1/2 tbsp of Garam masala.
  19. Prepare To taste of Salt.
  20. Prepare As needed of Ghee.
  21. It’s As needed of Oil.
  22. It’s of For omelet wrap :.
  23. Prepare 2 of large eggs.
  24. It’s 1 of pearl onion (shallot).
  25. It’s 1 of green chilli.
  26. It’s 5 of curry leaves.
  27. It’s 1 pinch of turmeric powder.
  28. It’s To taste of Salt.
  29. Prepare As needed of Oil.

Indi Sushi instructions

  1. Cook rice in a pot with all the ingredients under “for rice” with 1 1/2 cups of water..
  2. Heat oil in a pan and lightly roast the paneer cubes..
  3. Heat ghee in a wok, add ginger garlic paste and sauté it. And add onions, cook it until golden brown..
  4. Now add turmeric powder,coriander powder and garam masala and mix well. Fry it until the raw smell is gone..
  5. Further add chopped tomatoes and mix well. Now add red chilli powder and salt.mix well and cook covered until the tomatoes become mushy..
  6. Add in the grated coconut,combine well and add the cooked basmati rice..
  7. Combine well and adjust salt..
  8. Egg wrap : In a mixie jar add pearl onion (shallot/ small onion), green chilli, curry leaves, turmeric powder and salt. Grind it. Crack eggs into the jar and pulse for a couple of times..
  9. Heat oil in a pan and make one large omelet using the egg mixture..
  10. Cut the eggs as shown in the picture..
  11. Roll the omelet strips a shown, put a paneer chunk in the middle and fill the omelet roll with rice..
  12. Serve with ketchup, fruits and veggies..
