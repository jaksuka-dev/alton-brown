How to Make Yummy Spaghetti jollof

  • Alton Brown
  • Friday, Jan 31, 2020

Spaghetti jollof. Just showing you how I make this yummy Jollof Spaghetti. Making Jollof Spaghetti is almost the same as making the Nigerian Jollof Rice, I only add a little twist to this recipe to make it a more suited for Spaghetti. Note: My mixed vegetables consist of Carrots.

I had tried to make jollof spaghetti in the past, and it was not a success! It tasted okay, but it was missing that special “jollof-ness” for some reason. We Nigerians LOVE to ram it down the throats of. You can cook Spaghetti jollof using 8 ingredients and 3 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Spaghetti jollof

  1. It’s 1 sachet of spagetti.
  2. It’s of Fried fish.
  3. Prepare of Onions,papper,chilli.
  4. Prepare of Veg oil.
  5. You need of Green papper.
  6. Prepare of Seasoning.
  7. It’s of Chopped carrots.
  8. Prepare of Green beans.

Jollof spaghetti is spaghetti cooked in tomato sauce and spices just like one would cook jollof rice but using spaghetti instead. Some people also call it spaghetti jollof or Jollof pasta. Jollof Spaghetti or Spaghetti Jollof is a quick meal, that got it’s name from the popular Nigerian Jollof Rice. Jollof Spaghetti is quite easy to prepare and can be made with or without vegetables.

Spaghetti jollof step by step

  1. Boil water add spaghetti chopped carrots and grean beans,when soft drain in a colander.
  2. Blend pepper,onions,and chilli in a pot add a little oil fry and add the blended green pepper,seasoning and fish add the drained spagetti and some water, slice onions, spring onions and allow to cook.
  3. Serve hot.
