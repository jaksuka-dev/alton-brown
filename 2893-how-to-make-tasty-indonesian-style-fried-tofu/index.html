Indonesian Style Fried Tofu step by step

  1. Cut the tofu into small cubes. As small cubes your tofu will have more crunchy surfaces!.
  2. Dissolve the chicken bullion into hot water. Add the garlic powder, ground pepper, curcuma powder, and salty soya sauce..
  3. In a bowl, put the tofu and the water mix. Let soak for about 15 minutes..
  4. Bring oil and butter into heat in a pan. Fry the tofu in medium heat..
