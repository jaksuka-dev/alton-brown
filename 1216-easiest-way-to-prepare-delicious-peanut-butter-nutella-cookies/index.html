Peanut butter, Nutella cookies step by step

  1. First put your peanut butter and egg in a bowl and mix for 2 minutes.
  2. Next add your flour and keep mixing for another 3 minutes.
  3. After it’s all mixed wrap it in cling wrap and put it in the fridge for 15 minutes.
  4. Then when 15 minutes is done pick pieces off the ball and role them into tiny balls.
  5. Then poke wholes in them and add Nutella.
  6. After pre heat the oven to 350 and put it in the oven for 10 minutes.
  7. Then after 10 minutes you have to let them cool and enjoy.
