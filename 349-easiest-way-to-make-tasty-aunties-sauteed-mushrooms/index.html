Ingredients of Auntie’s Sautéed Mushrooms

  1. You need 2 packages of white button mushrooms; whole.
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp of butter.
  3. It’s 1 tsp of salt.
  4. You need 1 tsp of pepper.
  5. You need 2 tsp of garlic powder.
  6. It’s 1 tbsp of soy sauce.

Sauteed Mushrooms – perfectly tender, juicy mushrooms sauteed with garlic, thyme, onion, Creole seasoning and Worcestershire sauce. Best served as an appetizer or side dish to almost anything. These deliciously easy Sauteed Mushrooms and Onions make the perfect side, topping for grilled steak, or light dinner. This six ingredient recipe comes together quickly and tastes amazing.

Auntie’s Sautéed Mushrooms step by step

  1. Cut your mushrooms into 1/4” slices. (Or any thickness you like). Set aside..
  2. In a med saucepan over med high heat, melt the butter..
  3. Toss in your sliced mushrooms..
  4. Add the salt, pepper, garlic powder, and soy sauce. Fold over the cooked mushrooms to the top; repeat as necessary. Keep the pan uncovered..
  5. You’ll see a lot of liquid released from the mushrooms. This is GOLD..
  6. Cook to desired doneness. Enjoy! Don’t cook it for too long, they start shrinking!.
