How to Prepare Tasty Tortilla/Chapati Chiken Wrap

  • Alton Brown
  • Wednesday, Feb 5, 2020

Tortilla/Chapati Chiken Wrap.

You can cook Tortilla/Chapati Chiken Wrap using 10 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Tortilla/Chapati Chiken Wrap

  1. You need 1 of chicken Breast.
  2. It’s 1 of Cucumber.
  3. It’s 1 of assorted bell peppers each.
  4. It’s Half of cut Lettuce.
  5. You need 1 of onion.
  6. It’s of Grilled slices of pineapple.
  7. It’s 1 of Ovacado.
  8. You need of Ready made Tortilla/Chapati wrap.
  9. It’s of Salt & Pepper or add Cayenne to make it spicy.
  10. Prepare of Mint Ritta Sauce(Yorgurt+ Chopped Cucumber+Mint leaves).

Tortilla/Chapati Chiken Wrap instructions

  1. Season/Marinate your chicken as desired then roast it.
  2. Cut your Ovacado, lettuce, assorted pepper, cucumber and onions into julliennes cuts put them aside.
  3. Take the wrap then spread the Mint Ritta Sauce to make you wrap soft and tasty.
  4. Take you roast chicken cut into strips put in the middle of the wrap as well as the julliene cut vegs.
  5. Wrap then put on a hot pan for 10mins as you turn both sides.
  6. Serve it with your mint sauce and present it as desired.
