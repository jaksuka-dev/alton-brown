Recipe: Yummy Pan fried chicken with macarroni

  • Alton Brown
  • Thursday, Jun 11, 2020

Pan fried chicken with macarroni. Teaching How to make Fried Macaroni with chicken. Chicken Macaroni – it’s no secret kids love Macaroni like it’s the next best thing to chocolate. An instant classic and family favorite!

This easy chicken recipe turns out excellent every single time! To make this pan seared chicken, make sure to use thin boneless skinless chicken breasts. You can easily dredge them in the flour mixture. You can cook Pan fried chicken with macarroni using 6 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Pan fried chicken with macarroni

  1. Prepare 1/2 kg of chiken.
  2. It’s 1 of large onion.
  3. Prepare to taste of Salt.
  4. You need 1 of teaspoonful roycco.
  5. You need of Pounded garlic.
  6. Prepare 1 of chilli.

Chicken macaroni is a delicious dish that’s quite simple to prepare. This dish can be a one-pot meal. This Chicken macaroni recipe has a lot of health benefits as it contains several nutritious ingredients. The macaroni provides carbohydrates which serve as a major source of fuel for the body.

Pan fried chicken with macarroni instructions

  1. Boil 1/2kg of chicken in a sufuria.
  2. Add chili and pounded garlic to boil all together.
  3. When all water drains add cooking oil and stir.
  4. Add chopped onions and stir let it cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add salt and roycco then stir.
  6. Let it cook till golden brown.
  7. When done remove serve while hot with macarroni.

My chicken breasts came with chicken tenders attached. Cut each chicken breast in half. Most chicken breasts in the US are pretty big. You will end up with four pieces. It’s much easier to pan fry the chicken when the pieces are on the thinner side.
