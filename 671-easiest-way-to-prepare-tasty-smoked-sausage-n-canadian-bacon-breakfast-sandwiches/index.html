Smoked sausage n Canadian bacon Breakfast sandwiches step by step

  1. Bake biscuits as directed and allow to cook while you prepare the rest..
  2. Cook the smoked sausage until it’s crispy on both sides and set aside.
  3. Warm the Canadian bacon in the same skillet and set on plate with sausages..
  4. Crack your eggs and poke the yolks. Cook until firm..
  5. Open your biscuits midway through and assemble your sandwiches!.
