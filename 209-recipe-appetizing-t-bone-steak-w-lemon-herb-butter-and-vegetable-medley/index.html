Recipe: Appetizing T-Bone Steak w/Lemon Herb Butter and Vegetable Medley

T-Bone Steak w/Lemon Herb Butter and Vegetable Medley.

You can have T-Bone Steak w/Lemon Herb Butter and Vegetable Medley using 13 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of T-Bone Steak w/Lemon Herb Butter and Vegetable Medley

  1. You need of Lemon Herb Butter.
  2. Prepare 1 stick of Salted Butter, softened to room temperature.
  3. You need 1 tsp. of Lemon Juice.
  4. It’s 1/4 tsp. of Lemon Pepper Seasoning.
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp. of Cilantro Leaves, chopped.
  6. It’s 2 of T-Bone Steaks.
  7. It’s to taste of Salt and Pepper,.
  8. Prepare of Vegetable Medley.
  9. You need Half of a Head of Broccoli.
  10. You need Half of a Head of Cauliflower.
  11. Prepare 1 of / 2 Bag Frozen Green Peas.
  12. It’s 1/2 of Yellow Onion, chopped.
  13. Prepare 1 of / 2 Bag Green String Beans.

T-Bone Steak w/Lemon Herb Butter and Vegetable Medley instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F..
  2. In a small bowl mix together the butter ingredients. Mix well then set aside..
  3. Spray a rimmed cooking sheet with nonstick Cooking spray. Set aside. Season both sides of the steaks with salt and pepper. Sear steaks in a large skillet with olive oil bout 2-3 minutes per side. Then transfer to sprayed baking sheet and bake in oven for 16 minutes, flipping steaks over half way through..
  4. Mean while in the same skillet the steaks were seared in add a little bit of the lemon butter and 4 Tbsps. of water. Add all the vegetables and cook on medium high heat til vegetables are tender..
  5. Serve with 1/2tsp. Of butter mixture on top of the steaks with a healthy helping of the vegetable Medley..
