Easiest Way to Prepare Perfect Mayo Masala Bhutta

  • Alton Brown
  • Tuesday, Mar 17, 2020

Mayo Masala Bhutta.

You can cook Mayo Masala Bhutta using 6 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Mayo Masala Bhutta

  1. It’s 2 of bhutta/ corn/ sweet corn.
  2. Prepare 3 tbsp of Mayonnaise.
  3. It’s 1/2 cup of Grinded Kurkure.
  4. Prepare to taste of Salt.
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon of Chilli flakes.
  6. You need 1 teaspoon of Melted butter.

Mayo Masala Bhutta instructions

  1. Peel the bhutta and steam for 10 minutes.after steamed the Roasted the bhutta..
  2. Take a large bowl add mayonnaise,salt,chilli flakes melted butter.and mix it well..
  3. Now take a bhutta add mayonnaise spread.and sprinkle with grinded Kurkure..
  4. You can also added more spices if you like it..
  5. Now ready to serve..
