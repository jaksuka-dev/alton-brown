Ingredients of Cheesy Broccoli and Cauliflower Casserole

  1. It’s 1 of Large Head Broccoli.
  2. You need 1 of Large Head Cauliflower.
  3. You need 2 Cans of Cheddar Cheese Soup.
  4. Prepare 3/4 Cup of Milk.
  5. Prepare 3 tbsp of Dry Mustard.
  6. Prepare 1 of Sleeve Ritz Crackers.
  7. Prepare 2 Cups of Shredded Mild Cheddar.
  8. Prepare 2 cups of Shredded Sharp Cheddar.
  9. You need to taste of Salt and Pepper.

Cheesy Broccoli and Cauliflower Casserole instructions

  1. Preheat Oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Wash Veggies and chop into bite size pieces. Steam veggies slightly in pan of boiling water for 5-8 minutes.Place in large bowl. Set aside..
  3. Mix Soup, Milk, Dry Mustard, and Mild Cheddar in separate bowl. Salt and pepper to taste..
  4. Fold Soup/Cheese mixture into vegetables..
  5. Butter Large Casserole Dish and add Vegetable Mixture..
  6. Put Layer of Sharp Cheddar Over Vegetable Mix..
  7. Crush Sleeve of Ritz Crackers and Cover Top of Casserole..
  8. Bake for 30-45 minutes at 325. Broil for 2-4 minutes to Finish Browning Top..
