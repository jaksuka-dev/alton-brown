Ingredients of Tofu chicken nuggets

  1. It’s of Vegetable oil for frying.
  2. It’s 100 g of firm TOFU.
  3. It’s 300 g of (about 10 oz) Boneless Chicken Breast.
  4. It’s 1 tbsp of all-purpose flour.
  5. It’s 1 of egg.
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp of mayonnaise.
  7. You need 1/4 tsp of salt.
  8. You need 1 pinch of garlic powder.
  9. It’s 1 pinch of black pepper.

Who knew tofu ‘chicken’ nuggets could get so crispy without frying! Perfect with a big dollop of ketchup. These plant-based nuggets are made with smoked tofu and pasta. Tofu chicken “nuggets”: the perfect little finger food and guilty pleasure.

Tofu chicken nuggets step by step

  1. Cut Chicken Breast into small pieces..
  2. Combine Tofu, Chicken, flour, egg, mayonnaise, salt, garlic powder and pepper well until the texture becomes sticky. (You can add your favorite spice.).
  3. Heat 1 inch oil in a large frying pan over medium heat. When the oil is hot, use a spoon to drop the mixture..
  4. Pan fry until golden brown on each side..
  5. Serve with ketchup or your favorite sauce..
