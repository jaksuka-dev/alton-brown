Easiest Way to Prepare Perfect Sweet and Sour Tofu

  • Alton Brown
  • Saturday, Apr 11, 2020

Sweet and Sour Tofu. A plant-based version of this classic Chinese food that is much better and healthier than take out! Tofu is a staple in our kitchen, we use it a lot and also to make recipes for you guys. I recommend tripling or quadrupling the sauce recipe if you want that true restaurant type of sweet and sour that drips with sauce.

As you know, I love using the dust and quick fry method with tofu because it gives it a nice, crispy texture while keeping the cubes moist and silky on the inside. This Sweet and Sour Tofu is one of my favourite comfort foods ever of my favourite takeout! The tofu is crisp with a perfect texture and the sweet and sour is unbelievably delicious! You can cook Sweet and Sour Tofu using 5 ingredients and 2 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Sweet and Sour Tofu

  1. It’s 1 block of tofu.
  2. It’s Half of carrot.
  3. It’s Half of onion.
  4. You need of Sugar, ketchup, vinegar mixture.
  5. You need 2 pcs of tomato.

It comes together quickly and takes amazing as meal prep too! by: Jessica in the Kitchen. The sweet and sour sauce is sweet from the honey and ketchup and a touch “sour” (tart is really a better word for it) from the vinegar. It’s just perfectly salty enough from the soy sauce and it thickens into a delicious, sticky, caramelized coating on the tofu and vegetables when it comes together in the end. Heat the remaining oil in the pan over a high heat.

Sweet and Sour Tofu step by step

  1. Cut tofu, pat dry then coat with flour. Then fry it and set aside.
  2. Saute onion, carrot mix well then add tomato. Pour mixture simmer a little then add in fried tofu. Coat tofu with the sauce. Cook a little then remove.
