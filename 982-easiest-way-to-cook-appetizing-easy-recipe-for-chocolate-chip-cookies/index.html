Ingredients of Easy Recipe for Chocolate chip cookies

  1. You need 1/4 cup of sugar.
  2. You need 1/8 teaspoon of salt.
  3. It’s 1/4 cup of milk.
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon of baking soda.
  5. It’s 113 oz of unsalted butter (1 stick).
  6. You need 1 cup of flour.
  7. Prepare 1 of egg.
  8. Prepare 3/4 cup of chocolate chips (milk chocolate or semi sweet).

Easy Recipe for Chocolate chip cookies step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 360 degrees F.
  2. Soften butter to room temperature. Mix butter until fluffy and white. Add sugar to the butter and mix well..
  3. Add the egg. Mix well. Even if it doesn’t look right it is fine..
  4. Mix flour and baking soda and salt together. Then alternatively add the flour mixture and the milk to the sugar mixture. Start with flour and end with flour..
  5. Mix in chocolate chips..
  6. Put on parchment paper. Spread 2 inches apart from each other. Put in oven for 10-15 minutes. When done, cool for 5 min..
