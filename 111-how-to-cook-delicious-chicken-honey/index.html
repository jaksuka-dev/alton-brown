Ingredients of Chicken honey

  1. You need 8 of chicken drumsticks (skin on).
  2. You need 1 packet of Ready Sauce honey soy chicken (120g).
  3. You need 1/2 cup of dark brown sugar.
  4. You need 1/2 cup of hoisin sauce.
  5. You need 1/4 cup of barbecue sauce.
  6. It’s 1 tsp of Chinese five spice powder.
  7. Prepare 2 cloves of garlic (crushed).
  8. It’s 1 tbsp of ginger (grated).
  9. It’s 1/2 tsp of salt.
  10. It’s of GARNISHING INGREDIENTS (Optional).
  11. You need leaves of Mint.
  12. You need of Ginger (finely sliced).
  13. Prepare of Onions (finely sliced).

Chicken honey instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Combine all the ingredients in a mixing bowl, and transfer onto a baking paper lined oven tray. Roast for 45 minutes and baste often..
  2. Remove the chicken from the oven and strain the sauce into a saucepan. Boil until the sauce is thickened. Pour back over the chicken and roast for a further 15 minutes..
  3. Remove from the oven, garnish with mint leaves, finely sliced ginger and onions, and serve hot with steamed rice..
