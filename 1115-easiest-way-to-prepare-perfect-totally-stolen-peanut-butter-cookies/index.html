Try variations with chocolate chips or oatmeal, or even a no-bake version. These are truly the easiest peanut butter blossom cookies ever – just four ingredients. They turn out perfectly every time. You can have Totally Stolen Peanut Butter Cookies using 9 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Totally Stolen Peanut Butter Cookies

  1. It’s 1 of 1\4 Cups Firmly Packed Brown Sugar.
  2. It’s 3 of \4 Cup Smooth Peanut Butter.
  3. Prepare 1 of \2 Cup Vegetable Shortening.
  4. You need 1 of Large Egg.
  5. You need 3 Tablespoons of Milk.
  6. Prepare 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract.
  7. It’s 1 of 3\4 Cups AP Flour.
  8. It’s 3 of \4 Teaspoon Baking Soda.
  9. You need 1 of \2 Teaspoon Salt.

Have a glass of milk ready! How to Make Peanut Butter Cookies. Flourless Peanut Butter Cookies are for TRUE peanut butter fanatics! An easy peanut butter cookie recipe with so much peanut butter packed into each cookie!

Totally Stolen Peanut Butter Cookies step by step

  1. Mix first 6 ingredients together until smooth..
  2. Mix the 3 dry ingredients together until well combined..
  3. Add dry ingredients into the wet until mixture forms a smooth dough. A stand mixer works wonders here..
  4. Put dough balls on baking sheet. Roughly the size of a golf ball. Using a fork slightly press down making a criss cross pattern..
  5. Bake at 375" for 8-9 minutes. Don’t over bake. They may not look done, but they are. Cookies will be soft and chewy. Enjoy.
