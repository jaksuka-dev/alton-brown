Egg sauce step by step

  1. Wash and blend your bell pepper,onion and garlic.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a pan and add your paste.
  3. Cook for some minutes and add maggi,salt,curry and little water.
  4. Turn off the heat and set aside.
  5. Meanwhile,break and season your eggs with spices,salt and maggi and whisk with a hand mixer or whisk until it foams this makes it more fluffy.
  6. Heat oil in a pan,pour egg mixture and cook while using a wooden spoon to scramble the eggs.
  7. Pour the scrambled eggs into the sauce,add some sliced onions.
  8. Put the pan back on fire and cook for some minutes.
  9. Turn off heat and serve.
