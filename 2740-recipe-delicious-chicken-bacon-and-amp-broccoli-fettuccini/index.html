Ingredients of Chicken, Bacon & Broccoli Fettuccini

  1. It’s 2 pounds of chicken breast, cut into slices.
  2. You need 3 cloves of garlic, minced.
  3. It’s 1 1/3 cup of heavy cream.
  4. It’s 1 1/3 cup of milk.
  5. Prepare 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. It’s 1/2 cup of graded parmesan cheese.
  7. Prepare 2 cups of steamed broccoli floretes.
  8. Prepare 1 pound of bacon.
  9. You need 1 pound of fettuccini.

Chicken, Bacon & Broccoli Fettuccini instructions

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, add the minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute, then add in the cream and milk..
  2. Bring the garlic mixture to a boil, then add in the mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses and stir until melted.
  3. Turn off the heat and mix in the cooked pasta.
  4. Arrange the chicken slices, broccoli and bacon over the noodles. Fold everything together and serve..
