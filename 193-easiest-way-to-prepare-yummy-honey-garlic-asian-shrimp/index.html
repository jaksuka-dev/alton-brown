Ingredients of Honey garlic Asian shrimp

  1. It’s 1 lb of shrimp cleaned and tails removed.
  2. Prepare 1/3 cup of soy sauce.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of honey.
  4. You need 2 tsp of ground ginger.
  5. You need 1 tsp of crushed red pepper flakes.
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp of minced garlic.
  7. You need of scallions for garnish.
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp of Olive oil.

Honey garlic Asian shrimp step by step

  1. Mix all ingredients together except shrimp and scallions..
  2. Take half the marinade and marinade shrimp for about 20 minutes in fridge..
  3. Heat oil and cook shrimp on each side until cooked. Discard the marinade that the shrimp were in..
  4. After shrimp is cooked, cover with other half of marinade and let cook another couple of minutes..
  5. Serve over white rice and garnish with scallions. Enjoy!.
