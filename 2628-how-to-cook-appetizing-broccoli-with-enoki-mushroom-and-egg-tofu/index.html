Ingredients of Broccoli with enoki mushroom and egg tofu

  1. It’s 2 of egg tofu, cut into 1.5 cm and pan fry with a bit of oil until a slight crust develops on the tofu.
  2. You need 1 of medium size broccoli, cut into edible size florets.
  3. You need 1 packet of enoki mushroom (shiitake, oyster and button mushroom can also be used.
  4. Prepare 2 cloves of garlic, minced.
  5. It’s of About 100 gms of any kind of small seafood (squid, prawns or fishcake).
  6. Prepare to taste of White pepper and salt.
  7. You need 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce.
  8. You need 1 tablespoon of oil for stirfying.

Broccoli with enoki mushroom and egg tofu instructions

  1. Heat up oil in wok. Fry the minced garlic till fragrant..
  2. Add seafood, and stirfry till slightly cooked. Add about 1/2 cup water. Wait to simmer..
  3. Add a dash of white pepper, salt and oyster sauce..
  4. Add the broccoli florets and cooked till slightly tender before adding the enoki mushroom. Cook further until mushroom turns slightly soft..
  5. Add the tofu. Add a bit more water if needed for a bit of gravy. Stir to combine. Do not be to vigorous as we do not want the tofu to break..
  6. Enjoy the dish with white jasmine rice!.
  7. Taste for seasoning..
