Ingredients of Crockpot Honey-garlic chicken thighs

  1. It’s 8 of chicken thighs, remove skin.
  2. You need 1 C of soy sauce.
  3. Prepare 2/3 C of honey.
  4. It’s 1 C of ketchup.
  5. It’s 2 tsp of basil, dried.
  6. It’s 6 of garlic cloves, minced.
  7. Prepare of cooking spray.
