Easiest Way to Make Perfect Lemon Basil Chicken (Ayam Rica Rica)

  • Alton Brown
  • Thursday, Jul 23, 2020

Lemon Basil Chicken (Ayam Rica Rica).

You can cook Lemon Basil Chicken (Ayam Rica Rica) using 11 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Lemon Basil Chicken (Ayam Rica Rica)

  1. Prepare 1 kg of chicken. I use buffalo wings and drumstick.
  2. You need of ginger.
  3. Prepare of gallangal.
  4. Prepare leaves of Kaffir lime.
  5. Prepare of Lemongrass.
  6. It’s of Lime juice.
  7. It’s leaves of lemon basil.
  8. You need of Blended Spices.
  9. It’s 2 of shallots.
  10. You need 5 of garlics.
  11. It’s of Turmeric.

Lemon Basil Chicken (Ayam Rica Rica) step by step

  1. Wash the chicken, and add lime juice to remove the smell..
  2. Half fry the chicken and take it out from the pan..
  3. Blend the spices together until it smooth..
  4. Put a bit of oil in a hot pan, add the blended spices. Stir until the aroma is out. Add water and the rest of the spices and stir it. Put salt and sugar and check the taste..
  5. Once you’re happy with the seasoning, add chicken and stir it. Let the water absorb and wait until the water becomes pasty..
  6. Add the basil leaves, stir a bit, and turn off the stove..
