My grans special biscuits! instructions

  1. Pre heat oven 160c, prepare baking tray with baking paper on..
  2. Mix four and sugar in bowl, then rub in butter (room temperature)..
  3. Using a wooden spoon keep mixing until all bound together, keep kneading, put all onto floured board and have a rolling pin ready..
  4. Make a big ball shape and roll out to about 1cm – 1.5cm thick, using a cookie cutter make shapes and pop all shapes onto baking tray…. sprinkle with sugar….
  5. Add to middle shelf in oven, bake until a light golden brown. About 15/18 minutes. Leave to cool on cooling rack..
