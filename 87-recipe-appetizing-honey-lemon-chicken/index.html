Honey Lemon Chicken instructions

  2. Prepare the Chicken, Diced the Chicken And Add All the Marinade from the Ingridient List. Mix Well And Make Sure All The Chicken is Well Coated..
  3. Mix the Pre-Mix Sauce From Ingridient List, Except for the Slurry..
  4. Heat the Oil, And Fry the Chicken Until Cripsy & Golden Brown, Then Drain With a Strainer or Paper Towel. But Make Sure You Drain the Excess Oil As Much As Possible..
  5. Prepare the Sauce Pan & Cook the Pre-Mix Sauce. Then Add 2 Slices of Lemon. Test the Taste, if it to Sour, Feel Free to Add More Honey & Sugar Depending on Your Taste. I Love Sourness So Im not Adding More Sugar & Honey. ^_^ But if You Like Sweet Food, Just Add More Sugar & Honey..
  6. After The Sauce is Simmering, Remove the Lemon Slices & Add the Slurry Little by Little, And Adjust the Thickness You Want For the Sauce..
  7. Pour the Sauce Over the Fried Chicken, And Mix Them. Sprinkle Some Sesame Seeds & Green Onion. Put Over a Top of White Rice to Make a Rice Bowl. And Enjoy… ^_^.
