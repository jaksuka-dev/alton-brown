Easiest Way to Make Tasty Honey Habanero Chicken Sliders

  • Alton Brown
  • Sunday, May 3, 2020

Honey Habanero Chicken Sliders.

You can have Honey Habanero Chicken Sliders using 14 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Honey Habanero Chicken Sliders

  1. Prepare 6 pounds of raw chicken breast.
  2. You need 1 cup of water.
  3. It’s 1 cup of raw apple cider vinegar.
  4. Prepare 1 of and ½ cups raw honey.
  5. You need 1 of medium yellow onion chopped.
  6. You need 4 of large tomatoes washed and quartered.
  7. You need 4 of large cloves peeled garlic.
  8. Prepare 3 teaspoons of turmeric.
  9. It’s 3 teaspoons of ground mustard.
  10. It’s 3 teaspoons of sage.
  11. It’s 3 teaspoons of marjoram.
  12. It’s 3 teaspoons of thyme.
  13. Prepare 3 teaspoons of sea salt.
  14. Prepare 7 of whole washed Habanero peppers.

Honey Habanero Chicken Sliders instructions

  1. In a large saucepan bring everything but the raw chicken breast to a boil, then reduce to medium heat. Let cook and reduce by half. You will still have lots of liquid. Remove from the heat, allow to cool a bit. Onced cooled enough to handle, puree it all. Including those Habanero peppers. The heat becomes quite mellow because of the meat..
  2. Pour and coat the raw chicken breast completely. Cover and refrigerate 12 hours. *I used my slow cooker dish..
  3. Cook chicken in the marinade until done. Remove chicken, shred it, reserve some marinade. *Because I used my slow cooker dish, it was easy to pull it out and get it cooking..
  4. Take 2 cups of the marinade, put in a small saucepan. Bring to a boil, then lower heat to medium. Reduce liquid to half. Stir every few minutes. The marinade should coat your spoon. Pour this over your shredded chicken. If you like it saucy, use more than 2 cups. If you like it coated 2 cups is good..
  5. Beef and pork be great too, I used chicken because I already had it..
  6. I made buttermilk rolls for my sliders. Any roll will do..
