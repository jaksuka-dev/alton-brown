Easiest Way to Cook Appetizing Lemon Pound Cake

  • Alton Brown
  • Thursday, Sep 3, 2020

Lemon Pound Cake.

You can cook Lemon Pound Cake using 20 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Lemon Pound Cake

  1. It’s 1 3/4 c of all purpose flour.
  2. Prepare 1 1/4 c of sugar.
  3. Prepare 2 sticks of butter, softened.
  4. It’s 5 of eggs.
  5. Prepare 1/3 c of sour cream.
  6. It’s 1/2 tsp of baking powder.
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp of salt.
  8. Prepare of Zest from 3 lemons.
  9. It’s 3 tbsp of lemon juice.
  10. It’s 1/2 tsp of lemon extract.
  11. It’s 1/2 tsp of vanilla.
  12. You need of *3/4 cup of blueberries or 3tbsp poppyseeds-optional**.
  13. It’s of Lemon Simple Syrup.
  14. Prepare 1/4 c of sugar.
  15. It’s 1/4 c of fresh lemon juice.
  16. You need of Vanilla Glaze.
  17. You need 1/2 c of powdered sugar.
  18. Prepare 1 tbsp of milk.
  19. It’s 1/2 tsp of vanilla or vanilla bean.
  20. Prepare Pinch of salt.

Lemon Pound Cake step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 325 and lightly grease a 9×5 loaf pan with butter. Dust pan light with flour and set aside..
  2. In a bowl beat butter, sugar, lemon zest, lemon juice, and vanilla for 3 minutes until light and fluffy. Add sour cream until incorporated. Add eggs one at a time, mixing to combine after each addition..
  3. In another bowl whisk together flour, Baking powder and salt. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet ingredients. (Fold in blueberries or poppyseeds-optional). Toss blueberries lightly in flour before adding to batter..
  4. Bake 325 for 1 hour or until top is golden brown. Remove from oven and allow to cook for 20 mins. I’m a sauce pan, combine lemon juice and sugar. Once sugar has dissolved, allow to gently simmer for 2 minutes and remove from heat. Using a toothpick, poke cake lightly with holes all around cake. Brush syrup around top and sides of cake. Allow to cool for an hour before icing cake..
  5. Whisk together powder sugar vanilla bean, milk and salt. Slowly pour over cake and allow to sit 20 minutes to harden before serving..
