Ingredients of bake sushi

  1. Prepare 2 cup of sushi rice.
  2. You need 1 cup of sour cream.
  3. You need 1/2 packages of imitation crab cut to small pieces.
  4. You need 1/2 lb of shrimp cut to small pieces.
  5. It’s 1/2 cup of soft cream cheese.
  6. It’s 1/2 cup of mayonnaise.
  7. Prepare 3 tbsp of furikake aka Japanese rice seasoning.
  8. You need 3 tsp of salmon roe / fish egg.
  9. You need 1 packages of nori /sushi roll.

bake sushi step by step

  1. cook rice according to package direction.
  2. preheat oven to 350°F.
  3. mix in one bowl the shrimp, imitation crab, mayo, cream cheese and sour cream all together.
  4. in a baking dish layer cook rice, the mix shrimp and crab, rice seasoning and fish egg.
  5. bake for 15 mins.
  6. then cut 1 whole of nori to 4 pcs and serve.
  7. let your guests roll the bake sushi by them selves it’s more fun eating it.
