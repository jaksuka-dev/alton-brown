Chewy chocolate chip cookies. The Best Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies. There are a few secrets to the best classic, chewy chocolate chip cookies. Number one: Don’t use chips; instead, opt for a mix of milk or semisweet and dark chocolate chunks.

Use melted butter in this chocolate chip cookie recipe. Most of the cookies on my website call for creaming softened butter with the sugars. Make bakery-style chocolate chip cookies with these tips. You can have Chewy chocolate chip cookies using 8 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Chewy chocolate chip cookies

  1. It’s 1.5 cups of flour.
  2. Prepare 1 tsp of baking powder.
  3. It’s 1/2 cup of soft butter.
  4. It’s 2/3 cup of light brown sugar.
  5. You need 1/4 cup of white sugar.
  6. It’s 1/2 tsp of vanilla.
  7. You need 2 of eggs.
  8. Prepare of Tons of chocolate chips!.

Chewy chocolate chip cookies instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees.
  2. Cream warm butter. Add sugars. Then add vanilla and egg..
  3. Mix flour and baking powder in separate bowl then add slowly to wet mixture..
  4. Stir chocolate chips with spoon..
  5. Scoop tablespoons onto nonstick cookie sheet. Bake for 10-12 minutes. Cool before eating..
