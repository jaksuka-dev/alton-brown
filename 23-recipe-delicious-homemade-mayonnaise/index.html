Recipe: Delicious Homemade Mayonnaise

  • Alton Brown
  • Monday, Jul 13, 2020

Homemade Mayonnaise.

You can have Homemade Mayonnaise using 6 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Homemade Mayonnaise

  1. It’s 1 of egg.
  2. It’s 1 cup of oil.
  3. Prepare 1/8 teaspoon of salt.
  4. You need 1/8 teaspoon of white mustard (optional).
  5. It’s 1 Tablespoon of white vinegar or lime juice.
  6. You need 1 1/2 teaspoons of Dijon mustard.

Homemade Mayonnaise step by step

  1. Place all ingredients into a jar or mixing vessel with high sides..
  2. Mix thoroughly with an immersion blender, until all ingredients emulsify an look like mayonnaise. Normally for this small quantity, between 30 seconds and one minute..
  3. Voila, you have the simplest way to make mayonnaise at home. If you are worried about using raw eggs, which is necessary, Google how to pasteurize eggs–which can be done quickly in the microwave too :-).
  4. SUBSTITUTES and changes. You can use plain yellow mustard instead of Dijon. Mustard is used, not for flavor, but to emulsify the egg and oil-and skip it emulsified without separating. OLIVE oil can be used, in combination with whatever healthy cooking oil you have chose, try 1 part olive oil to two parts cooking oil to begin. All olive oil can be a bit overwhelming, depending on the oil. Adjust salt to taste. Adjust pepper to taste, or omit, to taste..
