Beef coconut curry instructions

  1. Slice onions and chop coriander stems. Fry until soft in a pan with ghee on medium heat..
  2. Finely chop garlic and ginger and grind with the spices in a mortar. Add to onions and cook until fragrant..
  3. Cut beef into 1 inch cubes. Raise heat on pan to high and add beef. Fry until beef is browned. Add 1 can coconut milk. Bring to a simmer..
  4. Put the curry in a pressure cooker for 45 mins..
  5. Return the beef from the pressure cooker to a pan and simmer on medium heat until reduced sufficiently..
  6. Serve the curry with the basmati rice and chopped coriander with a pinch of garam masala..
