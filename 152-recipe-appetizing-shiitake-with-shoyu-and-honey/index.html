Ingredients of Shiitake with Shoyu and Honey

  1. You need 250 gr of shiitake mushrooms, halved.
  2. You need 1 of onion, peeled, sliced thinly.
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp of oil.
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp of shoyu.
  5. It’s 1 tbsp of honey.
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp of water.
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp of chopped parsley.

Shiitake with Shoyu and Honey instructions

  1. Pour the oil into a pan. Using medium heat, stir-fry the onion until it caramelizes..
  2. Add shiitake mushrooms. Lower the heat and stir from time to time..
  3. When shiitake mushrooms have shrunk a bit, add the mixture of shoyu, honey, and water. Stir until well combined..
  4. Top with chopped parsley. And serve!.
