Ingredients of Mud Cake Muffin

  1. It’s 250 g of Unsalted Butter.
  2. You need 200 g of Dark Chocolate.
  3. You need 1+ 1/3 cup of Milk.
  4. Prepare 270 g (1 + 1/2 cup) of Castor Sugar.
  5. It’s 3/4 tsp of vanilla essence.
  6. It’s 2 of Eggs.
  7. It’s 230 g (1 + 1/2 +1/4 cup) of All Purpose Flour/Maida.
  8. You need 1 tsp of Baking Powder.
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp of Salt.
  10. You need 1/4 cup of Cocoa powder.

Mud Cake Muffin instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 C for 10 minutes. Prepare a muffin pan by applying butter.
  2. In a sauce pan, add butter, chocolate, milk and sugar..
  3. Mix until everything is melted. Let it cool to lukewarm..
  4. In another bowl, whisk in Eggs and add in melted mix and vanilla essence..
  5. Mix well, sift in the flour and cocoa powder..
  6. Mix till everything is smooth..
  7. Pour it in the prepared pan and bake for 12 to 15 min if muffin pan..
  8. Serve it with some fresh fruits or just dig in when it’s still hot!.
