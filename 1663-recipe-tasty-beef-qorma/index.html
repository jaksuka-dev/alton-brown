Beef qorma instructions

  1. Take big vessel and add oil in it.
  2. Thinly sliced onions and add into heated oil.
  3. Fry till golden brown.
  4. Take out half of fried onions and keep a side. Crushed them by hand.
  5. In the remain onions add all whole spices and meat.
  6. Fry meat till golden brown. Add ginger garlic paste and fry again.
  7. Add all dry spices and half of yoghurt mix till oil seperated.
  8. Now add water to let meat tender. Cover the lid and cook.
  9. Open the lid when meat become tender and dried out all the water..
  10. Add remaining yoghurt and mix with masala and meat.
  11. Fry till oil seperated again..
  12. Add crushed fried onions and mix for 1 – 2 minutes.
  13. Add water to make desired consistency of gravy. Cook till boil comes.
  14. Sprinkle whole spice powder on top and keep on simmer for 5 minutes.
  15. Dish out and serve with Naan. And enjoy with your family.
  16. You can use pressure cooker as well and increase or decrease ingredients according to your taste as well.
