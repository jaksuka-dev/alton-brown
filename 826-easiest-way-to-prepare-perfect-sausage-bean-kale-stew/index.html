Ingredients of Sausage, bean, kale stew

  1. You need 2 cup of white beans.
  2. Prepare 4 cups of chopped kale.
  3. You need 4 quarts of broth.
  4. You need 1 lb of hot or sweet sausage.
  5. You need 1 of shallot.
  6. You need 2 of long thin carrots, sliced into 2in circles.
  7. Prepare 1 of lg potatos, small diced.
  8. Prepare 2 tbl of oil.

Sausage, bean, kale stew step by step

  1. On medium heat in large pot heat oil then brown sausage.
  2. Mix in garlic and shallots then potatos and carrots, cook for 3 minutes.
  3. Mix in kale.
  4. Add broth and bring to boil.
  5. Cover and reduce to simmer for 15 minutes..
