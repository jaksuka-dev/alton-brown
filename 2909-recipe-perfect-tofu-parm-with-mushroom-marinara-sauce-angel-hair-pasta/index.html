Tofu parm with mushroom marinara sauce, angel hair pasta instructions

  1. Boil a pot of water, and cook pasta according to the instruction of the pasta box. Drain the cooked pasta and drizzle some olive oil, season with salt, pepper..
  2. In a wide bottomed pan,heat oil, fry the chopped onions until browned. Add garlic and mushrooms. Fry them until they are cooked..
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes and cook for 10 mins on medium heat. Season with salt, pepper. Add the chopped basil in the end., mix well and set it a side..
  4. In a bowl, whish the eggs..
  5. In the 2nd bowl, add the breadcrumbs, if plain ones add the italian seasonings to it, along with garlic powder, salt, pepper..
  6. Coat the tofu in egg bowl, then the bread crumbs and repeat once more to make sure it is coated well..
  7. Heat some oil in a pan on low to medium heat, fry the tofu well on both sides. In the end, add the cheese on top of the tofu block.
  8. Arrange the pasta, mushroom sauce,tofu on the plate and enjoy!!!.
