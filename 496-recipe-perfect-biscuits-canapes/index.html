It is relaxing and healthy snacks that no one can ignore, especially kids. You can cook Biscuits canapes using 9 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Biscuits canapes

  1. It’s 1 packet of Any salty biscuits.
  2. You need of Hung curd 1 /2 bowl.
  3. It’s of Capsicum.. 1/2tbsp chopped very small.
  4. It’s 1 tsp of Sugar.
  5. You need 1/2 tsp of salt.
  6. It’s of Oregano 1 /2 tsp.
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp of Black pepper.
  8. You need of Coriander leaves chopped fine 1 tbsp.
  9. You need of Peas boiled to decorate.

Monaco Biscuit Canapes are always a very. Canapes are also known as finger food. Canapes can be made in various ways using different bases, however the only thing I had that day were Monaco biscuits, so I made Biscuit Canapes. Monaco Biscuit Canapes – also known as Monaco Biscuit Toppings.

Biscuits canapes step by step

  1. Mix capsicum,coriander leaves, sugar,salt, oregano and black pepper into the hung curd.Mix them well..
  2. Spread this mix. on the surface of biscuits with the help of spoon..
  3. Decorate them with peas..
  4. Serve it as a starter..
  5. Biscuits should be crunchy and fresh..
  6. Hung curd should be of thick consistency..
