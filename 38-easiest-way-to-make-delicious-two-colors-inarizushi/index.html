Ingredients of Two colors Inarizushi

  1. You need 150 g of ground chicken.
  2. It’s 1 of table spoon sugar.
  3. Prepare 1 tablespoon of soysauce.
  4. You need 2 of eggs.
  5. You need 1 tablespoon of sugar.
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon of soysauce.
  7. You need 5 of seasoned deep fried bean curd (aburaage).
  8. It’s 1 cup of cooked rice.
  9. Prepare 3 of table spoons seasoned vinegar.

Inarizushi – sushi rice stuffed in sweet soy sauce flavored tofu pouches. Inarizushi is probably the easiest one to make. It is very popular with kids and is often placed into bento boxes. The modern version of Inarizushi is more decorative with colourful toppings, as you can see some of them in my photos.

Two colors Inarizushi step by step

  1. For chcken soboro : In a pot, combine ground chicken, sugar and Soy Sauce. At medium heat, cook chicken stirring constantly with 4 chopsticks until sauce is all evaporated..
  2. For egg soboro : In a pot, combine eggs,sugar and soy sauce. At medium heat, cook eggs stirring constantly with 4 chopstics..
  3. To make vinegered rice, put together cooked rice and seasoned vinegar in a small bowl..
  4. Place 5 seasoned aburaage on the plate. Seasoned aburaage is special to cook Inarizuhi..
  5. Put vinegared rice into seasoned aburaage..
  6. Put some eggs soboro on the left surface on the vinegared rice. Next put some chicken soboro on the right on the vinegared rice..
  7. Place two colors inarizushi on the plate..
