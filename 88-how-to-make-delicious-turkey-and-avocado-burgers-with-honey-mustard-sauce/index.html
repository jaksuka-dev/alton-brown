Turkey and avocado burgers with honey mustard sauce. Burgers made with ground turkey are a lean alternative to beef burgers, providing you choose turkey ground from the breast. Regular ground turkey, which is a mixture of light and dark meat and some skin, contains almost as much fat as lean ground beef. A honey-mustard mixture keeps these low-fat.

Ingredients of Turkey and avocado burgers with honey mustard sauce

  1. Prepare of Burger.
  2. Prepare 2 pounds of ground turkey.

  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp of ground pepper.
  6. You need 2 tsp of olive oil.
  7. It’s of Sauce.
  8. Prepare 1/3 cup of light mayonnaise.
  9. Prepare 2 tablespoons of honey mustard.
  10. You need of Toppings.
  11. Prepare 1 of large avocado sliced.
