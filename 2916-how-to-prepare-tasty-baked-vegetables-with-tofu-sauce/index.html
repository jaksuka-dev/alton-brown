How to Prepare Tasty Baked Vegetables with Tofu Sauce

  • Alton Brown
  • Friday, Apr 10, 2020

Baked Vegetables with Tofu Sauce. This Roasted Tofu is crunchy on the outside and creamy smooth-soft on the inside, with a wonderful flavor profile – slightly nutty, slightly sea salty. The browned bits are shatteringly crisp from roasting in the olive oil. Complete with a host of deliciously roasted vegetables, like sprouts, carrots and cauliflower.

You can also make this dish a tad healthier by using gluten free soy sauce and/ or low sodium ingredients. This time, I am going to introduce how to cook baked vegetables with tofu sauce. It is very easy and healthy. You can have Baked Vegetables with Tofu Sauce using 7 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Baked Vegetables with Tofu Sauce

  1. It’s 1 pack of soft tofu (400g).
  2. You need 1 tbsp of miso paste.
  3. Prepare 6 tbsp of mayonnaise.
  4. It’s of salt and pepper.
  5. It’s of An adequate amount of shredded cheese.
  6. You need of An adequate amount of any vegetables (e.g broccoli, cauliflower,.
  7. You need of pumpkin, mushroom).

You could get good-quality protein and a lot of vitamins both at once. Bake in the oven for about one hour, turning once, until tofu is golden and crispy. The edges should look crispy and glazed and the vegetables should be caramelized. Brush any extra sauce over top.

Baked Vegetables with Tofu Sauce instructions

  1. Cut the vegetables into bite-size chunks. Boil or steam them..
  2. Wrap the tofu with a kitchen towel and place it on a plate. Put it in the microwave for 2 minutes..
  3. Mix the tofu with mayo and miso. Add salt and pepper to taste..
  4. Put the vegetables on a baking dish. Pour the sauce over it.  Put an adequate amount of shredded cheese and bake for 25mins at 400°F until the surface has browned..
  5. Enjoy!.

Heat the rapeseed oil in a large wok or frying pan. Fry tofu for a few minutes per side, until brown and crispy, then transfer to a plate. Add all sauce ingredients to a bowl or mason jar and mix well to combine. Arrange roasted vegetables and tofu on a platter. Toss with the peanut sauce to serve.
