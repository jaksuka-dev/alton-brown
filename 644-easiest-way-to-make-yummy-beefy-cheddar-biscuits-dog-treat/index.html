Ingredients of Beefy Cheddar Biscuits (dog treat)

  1. Prepare 1 cup of rolled oats.
  2. It’s 5 tbsp of butter – cubed.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of boiling water.
  4. It’s 3/4 cup of cornmeal.
  5. Prepare 2 tsp of sugar.
  6. Prepare 2 tsp of beef bouillon granules.
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp of dried parsley (optional – helps with bad breath).
  8. It’s 1/2 cup of milk.
  9. It’s 1 cup of shredded cheddar.
  10. It’s 1 of egg – beaten.
  11. Prepare 3 cup of wheat flour.

Beefy Cheddar Biscuits (dog treat) instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 and line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper..
  2. In large bowl, pour boiling water over oats and butter. Stir slightly and let sit for 10-15 minutes..
  3. Stir in all remaining ingredients, except flour..
  4. Stir flour in, one cup at a time, until stiff dough forms. If dough is too sticky, add more flour..
  5. Knead dough, roll out on a floured surface to 1/4" thick and cut biscuits out..
  6. Knead scraps together, roll out, cut out biscuits and repeat until all dough is used..
  7. Place on baking sheets and bake for 35-40 minutes..
  8. Turn oven off and leave biscuits in oven for 20-30 minutes, allowing them to dry and crisp..
