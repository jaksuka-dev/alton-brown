Recipe: Tasty Banana Pancakes (no flour)

  • Alton Brown
  • Monday, May 18, 2020

Banana Pancakes (no flour). Hello guys, Sharing with you today this super easy recipe to make banana pancakes instantly. All you need is banana and eggs, mash them together with a. large egg, banana, almond flour, cinnamon, coconut oil. These two-ingredient banana pancakes have been floating around the internet for several years now, first on fitness sites (protein! low fat!) and then on parenting sites Needless to say, I’m hooked.

This banana pancakes have very light texture because there is no flour in it. These healthy banana oatmeal pancakes are just that: made mainly of oats and bananas (no flour!) and naturally sweet with maple syrup. I’ll just say it: pancakes are an excuse to eat cake for breakfast. You can cook Banana Pancakes (no flour) using 3 ingredients and 8 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Banana Pancakes (no flour)

  1. You need 1 of Banana.
  2. You need 2 of Eggs.
  3. Prepare of Cinnamon + Sugar (Optional).

At this point in my life, a huge dose of sugar and refined flour in the morning doesn’t make. Crowd pleasing banana pancakes made from scratch. A fun twist on ordinary pancakes. Banana pancakes are incredibly easy to make, especially when you’re making them for two, because you just need one overripe banana and a handful of pantry staples.

Banana Pancakes (no flour) step by step

  1. Ingredients.
  2. Peel and slice the bananas.
  3. Put them in a bowl with the eggs..
  4. Mash the bananas and whisk the Eggs until they blend together..
  5. Add cinnamon and sugar to the mix, to your taste..
  6. Put some of the mix in a hot pan. Wait a couple of minutes and flip it.
  7. Keep doing it until you go through all the mix. (3 pancakes for me).
  8. You can eat it with syrup or put some jelly or peanut butter and roll them up.

You whisk together the dry ingredients: flour, a little sugar, baking powder, and salt. Then in a separate bowl, mash your banana. Fluffy grain-free banana pancakes that are rich in fiber + protein. The secret lies in a little baking powder and coconut flour, which keep these pancakes grain-free and incredibly cake-like in texture! The search for the perfect Paleo pancake continues!
