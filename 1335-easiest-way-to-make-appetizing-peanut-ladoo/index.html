Ingredients of Peanut ladoo

  1. It’s 1 cup of peanuts.
  2. You need 1 cup of sugar.
  3. It’s 1/4 cup of milk powder.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of water.
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon of cardamom powder.
  6. You need 1 tablespoon of Khaman for coating ladoos.
  7. Prepare as needed of Yellow colour.
  8. You need 1 tablespoon of Ghee.
  9. It’s Handful of almonds.

Peanut ladoo instructions

  1. Firstly roast peanuts nicely and grind them into fine powder.
  2. Also grind almond to make fine powder.
  3. Further mix almond powder and milk powder into peanut powder.
  4. Prepare sugar syrup add water and sugar by adding food colour and cardamom powder.
  5. Now add peanut mixture into this with ghee and stir for 4 to 5 minutes,transfer and prepare ladoos when warm.
