How to Cook Perfect Egg Roll

Egg Roll. Egg rolls are a variety of deep-fried appetizers served in American Chinese restaurants. An egg roll is a cylindrical, savory roll with shredded cabbage, chopped pork. Learn how to make egg rolls right at home!

These homemade egg rolls are filled with pork and vegetables, all wrapped up and fried to crispy perfection. The perfect make-ahead appetizer for any event! You can cook Egg Roll using 11 ingredients and 9 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Egg Roll

  1. It’s 2 of eggs.
  2. You need 1 cup of Maida.
  3. You need 1 of medium size onion chopped fine.
  4. You need 1 of medium size tomato chopped fine.
  5. You need 1 of small size capsicum chopped fine.
  6. It’s 1 of lime juice.
  7. It’s 1 Tbsp of Chaat Masala.
  8. You need 2-3 of green chillies chopped fine.
  9. You need 1 tsp of black pepper powder.
  10. You need as required of Oil to fry.
  11. Prepare to taste of Salt.

Egg rolls are a quintessential Chinese takeout appetizer. I used an eight inch iron skillet that worked out perfectly. Egg rolls are a classic appetizer at Chinese restaurants, but there are also many simple simple varieties to make at home yourself and enjoy anytime. Baking egg rolls definitely creates a different texture than frying, but I still liked them A LOT.

Egg Roll step by step

  1. In a bowl add Maida and with 1/2 tsp oil and salt make a dough like for Roti. Keep it covered for 10 mins with a muslin cloth.
  2. Chop onions, tomatoes, green capsicum and set aside..
  3. Beat a egg and add black pepper powder, salt and beat well..
  4. With a rolling pin make a Roti..
  5. In a tawa heat oil, add the egg and spread. On low flame cook for 1/2 a min. Then add the Roti. Press with a spatula and let the egg set in..
  6. Flip the other side and let the Roti cook. Set aside.
  7. On a kitchen counter place the Roll, place the chopped vegetables in the centre, add Chaat masala, black pepper powder, salt, lime juice..
  8. Roll and cover half with a parchment paper.
  9. Plate and enjoy hot.
