Recipe: Tasty Oven Roasted Broccoli

  • Alton Brown
  • Tuesday, Apr 14, 2020

Oven Roasted Broccoli. Mix together the oil, garlic, red pepper flakes, salt and pepper. Toss together the broccoli and the oil mixture in a large bowl. Line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper* or mist with cooking spray.

Broccoli florets are roasted after being tossed in olive oil and sprinkled with sea salt, freshly ground black pepper, and minced garlic. A squeeze of lemon juice before serving seals the deal. Toss the broccoli florets with the olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper on a baking sheet. You can cook Oven Roasted Broccoli using 6 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Oven Roasted Broccoli

  1. It’s 6 of crowns of fresh broccoli or about 6 to 8 cups.
  2. It’s 3 Tbsp. of olive oil.
  3. It’s 2 tsp. of garlic powder.
  4. You need 1 tsp. of black pepper.
  5. You need 2 tsp. of McCormick Spicy Steak Seasoning.
  6. You need 1 gallon of size ziplock bag.

Spread them out and then roast, without stirring, until the edges are. Cut the broccoli florets from the thick stalks, leaving an inch or two of stalk attached to the florets, discarding the rest of the stalks. Combine the broccoli, olive oil, salt, and a few grinds of pepper in a large bowl and toss to coat. Carefully remove the hot baking sheet from the oven, add the broccoli, and spread it into an even layer.

Oven Roasted Broccoli step by step

  1. Wash broccoli and cut off tops..
  2. Place pieces into the ziplock bag. Add all seasonings and olive oil. Give it a good shake to disperse oil and seasonings..
  3. Spread out on a large baking sheet and bake for 10 to 15 minutes in oven at 400 degrees..
  4. Enjoy!!.

Cut the broccoli florets into bite size pieces. Oven Roasted Broccoli is a nutritious side dish your family will love! It pairs well with so many meals. It’s a perfect side dish for chicken, beef, fish, and pasta. In my home, we love serving it with my Simple Oven Roasted Chicken Thighs for a quick weeknight meal.
