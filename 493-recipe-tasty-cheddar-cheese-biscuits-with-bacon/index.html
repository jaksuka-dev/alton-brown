Ingredients of Cheddar cheese Biscuits with Bacon

  1. You need of Biscuit————-.
  2. Prepare 4 cup of self-rising flour.
  3. It’s 1/4 cup of oil.
  4. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of granulated garlic powder.
  5. Prepare 1-3/4 cup of milk.
  6. You need 1/4 cup of chopped parsley.
  7. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon of salt.
  8. It’s 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese.
  9. Prepare of Bacon—————-.
  10. Prepare 1/3 pound of Bacon sliced.
  11. It’s of Butter—————-.
  12. Prepare 1/2 cup of butter.
  13. Prepare 1 teaspoon of granulated garlic powder.

Cheddar cheese Biscuits with Bacon instructions

  1. Preheat oven 450 degrees Fahrenheit. Add the garlic to the butter and melt. Mix well..
  2. Mix the flour, oil, parsley, salt, 1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic powder, and milk. Knead into a ball and add the cheese.
  3. Mix well..
  4. Form into balls and flatten them heat a skillet add to the skillet. Put into the oven for 12-17 minutes..
  5. Fry the bacon crisp move to a paper towel..
  6. Biscuits are done..
  7. Stir the butter garlic mixture and brush on top of the biscuits..
  8. Split open add a light brush of garlic butter..
  9. Add the bacon..
  10. Serve I hope you enjoy!!.
