How to Prepare Tasty Homemade Pepperoni Pizza (using Biscuits)

  • Alton Brown
  • Wednesday, Aug 5, 2020

Homemade Pepperoni Pizza (using Biscuits).

You can cook Homemade Pepperoni Pizza (using Biscuits) using 4 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Homemade Pepperoni Pizza (using Biscuits)

  1. Prepare 1 can of Buttermilk biscuits (brand is optional).
  2. You need 1 packages of Mozzarella shredded cheese.
  3. Itâ€™s 1 of Marinara sauce.
  4. You need 1 of Garlic and onion salt.

Homemade Pepperoni Pizza (using Biscuits) step by step

  1. 1: Preset oven to 375. While the stove is heating up open your can of biscuits ( I used the 8 count). Mash each biscuit one by one (as if your making hamburgers)..
  2. 2: Place mashed biscuits in a non stick cooking pan side by side. I merged the biscuits together to make the base of the pizza. (No holes should be seen).
  3. 3: Place biscuits in the oven for 10 minutes..
  4. 4: Take the biscuits out the oven after ten min (should have a light brown coat on them). Now it’s time to add your marinara sauce, cheese, and pepperoni..
  5. 5: Add the marinara sauce first. Cover the Base with marinara sauce to your liking. Then sprinkle your shredded mozzarella cheese to your liking as well. Adding garlic and onion salt is optional But I didn’t add any this time. Lastly add your pepperoni and use as much as you like..
  6. 6: Place cooking pan back in the oven and let the pizza to cook for another 10-12 minutes. After the pizza is fully cook take it out the oven and enjoy!!!.
