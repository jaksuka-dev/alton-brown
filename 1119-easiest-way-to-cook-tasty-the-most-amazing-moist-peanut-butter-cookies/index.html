Ingredients of The most amazing moist peanut butter cookies!

  1. Prepare 1 stick of butter (softened).
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of smooth peanut butter.
  3. You need 1 1/4 cup of white flour.
  4. It’s 1/2 cup of granulated sugar.
  5. You need 1/2 of light brown sugar (or 1/4 cup honey).
  6. Prepare 1 of egg (room temperature).
  7. It’s 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda.
  8. It’s 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder.
  9. You need 1 teaspoon of vanilla.
  10. Prepare of White sugar (separate).

The most amazing moist peanut butter cookies! step by step

  1. Mix together (with an electric mixer) butter and peanut butter until smooth.
  2. Add 1/2 cup flour, both sugars, baking soda, egg, baking powder, and vanilla. Mix well.
  3. Make your dough into balls and place on an un greased cookie sheet. Then roll in white sugar.
  4. Make a cross cross mark with a fork..
  5. Bake at 375 degrees for 7-9 minutes.
