Ingredients of 30 minute Magic cookie bars

  1. Prepare 1.5 cups of graham crackers crumbs.
  2. You need 1/2 cup of soft or melted butter.
  3. It’s 1-14 oz of can sweetened condensed milk.
  4. It’s 3/4 cup of butterscotch chips.
  5. You need 3/4 cup of chocolate chips.
  6. It’s 3/4 cup of white chocolate chips.
  7. Prepare 1.5 cups of shredded sweetened coconut.
  8. You need 1 cup of chopped walnuts.

30 minute Magic cookie bars instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350..
  2. Mix butter and graham cracker crumbs in mixing bowl and press onto a greased or parchment paper lined 13×9” baking pan. Pour condensed milk evenly over it..
  3. Spread all 3 kids of chips over graham crackers and press down a little bit into the crust..
  4. Sprinkle walnuts and coconut evenly on top and press down as well. Bake 20 minutes or until edges are a golden brown. Allow to cool for an hour and cut into squares. You can eat as is or place in fridge to firm up even more..
